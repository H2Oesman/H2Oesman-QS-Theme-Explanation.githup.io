Niat (Intention) for Prayers

Note: The Niat (intention) is made in the heart before beginning the prayer. Uttering it verbally is sunnah (recommended) to help the heart stay focused. Below are the standard formulas for Fard, Sunnah, Qasr (shortening), and Jam' (combining) prayers.

Niat for the 5 Fard Prayers Fard

Fard (Obligatory) — These are the 5 daily prayers that every Muslim must perform. Each has a specific number of Rak'ahs.
1. Fajr (Subuh) — 2 Rak'ahs Fard
أُصَلِّي فَرْضَ الصُّبْحِ رَكْعَتَيْنِ أَدَاءً لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى
Uṣallī farḍa aṣ-ṣubḥi rak'atayni adā'an lillāhi ta'ālā
I intend to perform the obligatory Fajr prayer, two rak'ahs, for the sake of Allah Ta'ala.
2. Dhuhr (Dzuhur) — 4 Rak'ahs Fard
أُصَلِّي فَرْضَ الظُّهْرِ أَرْبَعَ رَكَعَاتٍ أَدَاءً لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى
Uṣallī farḍa aẓ-ẓuhri arba'a rak'ātin adā'an lillāhi ta'ālā
I intend to perform the obligatory Dhuhr prayer, four rak'ahs, for the sake of Allah Ta'ala.
3. Asr — 4 Rak'ahs Fard
أُصَلِّي فَرْضَ الْعَصْرِ أَرْبَعَ رَكَعَاتٍ أَدَاءً لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى
Uṣallī farḍa al-'aṣri arba'a rak'ātin adā'an lillāhi ta'ālā
I intend to perform the obligatory Asr prayer, four rak'ahs, for the sake of Allah Ta'ala.
4. Maghrib — 3 Rak'ahs Fard
أُصَلِّي فَرْضَ الْمَغْرِبِ ثَلَاثَ رَكَعَاتٍ أَدَاءً لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى
Uṣallī farḍa al-maghribi thalātha rak'ātin adā'an lillāhi ta'ālā
I intend to perform the obligatory Maghrib prayer, three rak'ahs, for the sake of Allah Ta'ala.
5. Isha (Isya) — 4 Rak'ahs Fard
أُصَلِّي فَرْضَ الْعِشَاءِ أَرْبَعَ رَكَعَاتٍ أَدَاءً لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى
Uṣallī farḍa al-'ishā'i arba'a rak'ātin adā'an lillāhi ta'ālā
I intend to perform the obligatory Isha prayer, four rak'ahs, for the sake of Allah Ta'ala.

Niat for Sunnah Prayers Sunnah

Sunnah (Recommended) — These are the voluntary prayers performed before (Qabliyah) or after (Ba'diyah) the Fard prayers. They bring great reward and compensate for any shortcomings in the obligatory prayers.
Fajr — 2 Rak'ahs Qabliyah
أُصَلِّي سُنَّةَ الصُّبْحِ رَكْعَتَيْنِ قَبْلِيَّةً لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى
Uṣallī sunnata aṣ-ṣubḥi rak'atayni qabliyyatan lillāhi ta'ālā
I intend to perform the Sunnah of Fajr, two rak'ahs before (the Fard), for the sake of Allah Ta'ala.
Dhuhr — 4 Rak'ahs Qabliyah
أُصَلِّي سُنَّةَ الظُّهْرِ أَرْبَعَ رَكَعَاتٍ قَبْلِيَّةً لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى
Uṣallī sunnata aẓ-ẓuhri arba'a rak'ātin qabliyyatan lillāhi ta'ālā
I intend to perform the Sunnah of Dhuhr, four rak'ahs before (the Fard), for the sake of Allah Ta'ala.
Dhuhr — 2 Rak'ahs Ba'diyah
أُصَلِّي سُنَّةَ الظُّهْرِ رَكْعَتَيْنِ بَعْدِيَّةً لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى
Uṣallī sunnata aẓ-ẓuhri rak'atayni ba'diyyatan lillāhi ta'ālā
I intend to perform the Sunnah of Dhuhr, two rak'ahs after (the Fard), for the sake of Allah Ta'ala.
Asr — 4 Rak'ahs Qabliyah
أُصَلِّي سُنَّةَ الْعَصْرِ أَرْبَعَ رَكَعَاتٍ قَبْلِيَّةً لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى
Uṣallī sunnata al-'aṣri arba'a rak'ātin qabliyyatan lillāhi ta'ālā
I intend to perform the Sunnah of Asr, four rak'ahs before (the Fard), for the sake of Allah Ta'ala.
Maghrib — 2 Rak'ahs Ba'diyah
أُصَلِّي سُنَّةَ الْمَغْرِبِ رَكْعَتَيْنِ بَعْدِيَّةً لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى
Uṣallī sunnata al-maghribi rak'atayni ba'diyyatan lillāhi ta'ālā
I intend to perform the Sunnah of Maghrib, two rak'ahs after (the Fard), for the sake of Allah Ta'ala.
Isha — 2 Rak'ahs Ba'diyah
أُصَلِّي سُنَّةَ الْعِشَاءِ رَكْعَتَيْنِ بَعْدِيَّةً لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى
Uṣallī sunnata al-'ishā'i rak'atayni ba'diyyatan lillāhi ta'ālā
I intend to perform the Sunnah of Isha, two rak'ahs after (the Fard), for the sake of Allah Ta'ala.

Niat for Qasr (Shortened) Prayers Qasr

Qasr (Shortening) — When traveling (safar), it is highly recommended to shorten the 4-Rak'ah prayers (Dhuhr, Asr, Isha) to 2 Rak'ahs each. This is a concession from Allah to ease the journey. The niyyah includes the word قَصْرًا (qasran).
Dhuhr — 2 Rak'ahs Qasr
أُصَلِّي فَرْضَ الظُّهْرِ رَكْعَتَيْنِ قَصْرًا لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى
Uṣallī farḍa aẓ-ẓuhri rak'atayni qaṣran lillāhi ta'ālā
I intend to perform the obligatory Dhuhr prayer, shortened to two rak'ahs, for the sake of Allah Ta'ala.
Asr — 2 Rak'ahs Qasr
أُصَلِّي فَرْضَ الْعَصْرِ رَكْعَتَيْنِ قَصْرًا لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى
Uṣallī farḍa al-'aṣri rak'atayni qaṣran lillāhi ta'ālā
I intend to perform the obligatory Asr prayer, shortened to two rak'ahs, for the sake of Allah Ta'ala.
Isha — 2 Rak'ahs Qasr
أُصَلِّي فَرْضَ الْعِشَاءِ رَكْعَتَيْنِ قَصْرًا لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى
Uṣallī farḍa al-'ishā'i rak'atayni qaṣran lillāhi ta'ālā
I intend to perform the obligatory Isha prayer, shortened to two rak'ahs, for the sake of Allah Ta'ala.

Note: Maghrib (3 Rak'ahs) and Fajr (2 Rak'ahs) are not shortened during travel — only the 4-Rak'ah prayers are reduced to 2.

Niat for Jam' Taqdim جمع تقديم

Jam' Taqdim (Combining Early) — When traveling or in valid circumstances, you may combine two prayers at the time of the first prayer. For Dhuhr + Asr, perform both at Dhuhr time. For Maghrib + Isha, perform both at Maghrib time. The niyyah for the second prayer includes مَجْمُوعًا إِلَى ... جَمْعَ تَقْدِيمٍ.
Dhuhr + Asr — Jam' Taqdim Standard (4+4)
1. Niat for Dhuhr (normal)
أُصَلِّي فَرْضَ الظُّهْرِ أَرْبَعَ رَكَعَاتٍ أَدَاءً لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى
Uṣallī farḍa aẓ-ẓuhri arba'a rak'ātin adā'an lillāhi ta'ālā
I intend to perform the obligatory Dhuhr prayer, four rak'ahs, for the sake of Allah Ta'ala.
2. Niat for Asr (combined)
أُصَلِّي فَرْضَ الْعَصْرِ أَرْبَعَ رَكَعَاتٍ مَجْمُوعًا إِلَى الظُّهْرِ جَمْعَ تَقْدِيمٍ لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى
Uṣallī farḍa al-'aṣri arba'a rak'ātin majmū'an ilā aẓ-ẓuhri jam'a taqdīmin lillāhi ta'ālā
I intend to perform the obligatory Asr prayer, four rak'ahs, combined with Dhuhr (at Dhuhr time) — Jam' Taqdim, for the sake of Allah Ta'ala.
Maghrib + Isha — Jam' Taqdim Standard (3+4)
1. Niat for Maghrib (normal)
أُصَلِّي فَرْضَ الْمَغْرِبِ ثَلَاثَ رَكَعَاتٍ أَدَاءً لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى
Uṣallī farḍa al-maghribi thalātha rak'ātin adā'an lillāhi ta'ālā
I intend to perform the obligatory Maghrib prayer, three rak'ahs, for the sake of Allah Ta'ala.
2. Niat for Isha (combined)
أُصَلِّي فَرْضَ الْعِشَاءِ أَرْبَعَ رَكَعَاتٍ مَجْمُوعًا إِلَى الْمَغْرِبِ جَمْعَ تَقْدِيمٍ لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى
Uṣallī farḍa al-'ishā'i arba'a rak'ātin majmū'an ilā al-maghribi jam'a taqdīmin lillāhi ta'ālā
I intend to perform the obligatory Isha prayer, four rak'ahs, combined with Maghrib (at Maghrib time) — Jam' Taqdim, for the sake of Allah Ta'ala.

Dhuhr + Asr — Jam' Taqdim with Qasr 2+2 Raka'at
1. Niat for Dhuhr (Qasr)
أُصَلِّي فَرْضَ الظُّهْرِ رَكْعَتَيْنِ قَصْرًا لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى
Uṣallī farḍa aẓ-ẓuhri rak'atayni qaṣran lillāhi ta'ālā
I intend to perform the obligatory Dhuhr prayer, shortened to two rak'ahs, for the sake of Allah Ta'ala.
2. Niat for Asr (Qasr + Combined)
أُصَلِّي فَرْضَ الْعَصْرِ رَكْعَتَيْنِ قَصْرًا مَجْمُوعًا إِلَى الظُّهْرِ جَمْعَ تَقْدِيمٍ لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى
Uṣallī farḍa al-'aṣri rak'atayni qaṣran majmū'an ilā aẓ-ẓuhri jam'a taqdīmin lillāhi ta'ālā
I intend to perform the obligatory Asr prayer, shortened to two rak'ahs, combined with Dhuhr (at Dhuhr time) — Jam' Taqdim, for the sake of Allah Ta'ala.
Maghrib + Isha — Jam' Taqdim with Qasr 3+2 Raka'at
1. Niat for Maghrib (normal)
أُصَلِّي فَرْضَ الْمَغْرِبِ ثَلَاثَ رَكَعَاتٍ أَدَاءً لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى
Uṣallī farḍa al-maghribi thalātha rak'ātin adā'an lillāhi ta'ālā
I intend to perform the obligatory Maghrib prayer, three rak'ahs, for the sake of Allah Ta'ala.
2. Niat for Isha (Qasr + Combined)
أُصَلِّي فَرْضَ الْعِشَاءِ رَكْعَتَيْنِ قَصْرًا مَجْمُوعًا إِلَى الْمَغْرِبِ جَمْعَ تَقْدِيمٍ لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى
Uṣallī farḍa al-'ishā'i rak'atayni qaṣran majmū'an ilā al-maghribi jam'a taqdīmin lillāhi ta'ālā
I intend to perform the obligatory Isha prayer, shortened to two rak'ahs, combined with Maghrib (at Maghrib time) — Jam' Taqdim, for the sake of Allah Ta'ala.

Niat for Jam' Ta'khir جمع تأخير

Jam' Ta'khir (Combining Late) — Combine two prayers at the time of the second prayer. For Dhuhr + Asr, perform both at Asr time. For Maghrib + Isha, perform both at Isha time. The niyyah for the first prayer includes مَجْمُوعًا إِلَى ... جَمْعَ تَأْخِيرٍ.
Dhuhr + Asr — Jam' Ta'khir Standard (4+4)
1. Niat for Dhuhr (combined)
أُصَلِّي فَرْضَ الظُّهْرِ أَرْبَعَ رَكَعَاتٍ مَجْمُوعًا إِلَى الْعَصْرِ جَمْعَ تَأْخِيرٍ لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى
Uṣallī farḍa aẓ-ẓuhri arba'a rak'ātin majmū'an ilā al-'aṣri jam'a ta'khīrin lillāhi ta'ālā

أُصَلِّي فَرْضَ الْعَصْرِ أَرْبَعَ رَكَعَاتٍ أَدَاءً لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى

Niat Sholat

Catatan: Niat diucapkan dalam hati sebelum memulai sholat. Mengucapkannya secara lisan adalah sunnah (dianjurkan) untuk membantu hati tetap fokus. Di bawah ini adalah formula standar untuk sholat Fardhu, Sunnah, Qashar (memendekkan), dan Jama' (menggabungkan).

Lompat ke: ?Sunnah ?Qashar ?Taqdim ?Takhir

Niat 5 Sholat Fardhu Fardhu

Fardhu (Wajib) — Ini adalah 5 sholat harian yang wajib dilakukan oleh setiap Muslim. Masing-masing memiliki jumlah raka'at tertentu.
1. Subuh — 2 Raka'at Fardhu
أُصَلِّي فَرْضَ الصُّبْحِ رَكْعَتَيْنِ أَدَاءً لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى
Ushallii fardha ash-subhi raka'ataini adaan lillahi ta'aalaa
Saya niat sholat fardhu Subuh dua raka'at, karena Allah Ta'ala.
2. Dzuhur — 4 Raka'at Fardhu
أُصَلِّي فَرْضَ الظُّهْرِ أَرْبَعَ رَكَعَاتٍ أَدَاءً لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى
Ushallii fardha azh-zhuhri arba'a raka'atin adaan lillahi ta'aalaa
Saya niat sholat fardhu Dzuhur empat raka'at, karena Allah Ta'ala.
3. Ashar — 4 Raka'at Fardhu
أُصَلِّي فَرْضَ الْعَصْرِ أَرْبَعَ رَكَعَاتٍ أَدَاءً لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى
Ushallii fardha al-'ashri arba'a raka'atin adaan lillahi ta'aalaa
Saya niat sholat fardhu Ashar empat raka'at, karena Allah Ta'ala.
4. Maghrib — 3 Raka'at Fardhu
أُصَلِّي فَرْضَ الْمَغْرِبِ ثَلَاثَ رَكَعَاتٍ أَدَاءً لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى
Ushallii fardha al-maghribi thalaatha raka'atin adaan lillahi ta'aalaa
Saya niat sholat fardhu Maghrib tiga raka'at, karena Allah Ta'ala.
5. Isya — 4 Raka'at Fardhu
أُصَلِّي فَرْضَ الْعِشَاءِ أَرْبَعَ رَكَعَاتٍ أَدَاءً لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى
Ushallii fardha al-'ishaa-i arba'a raka'atin adaan lillahi ta'aalaa
Saya niat sholat fardhu Isya empat raka'at, karena Allah Ta'ala.

Niat Sholat Sunnah Sunnah

Sunnah (Anjuran) — Sholat-sholat sunnah yang dikerjakan sebelum (Qabliyah) atau sesudah (Ba'diyah) sholat fardhu. Mendatangkan pahala besar dan menutupi kekurangan dalam sholat wajib.

Ushallii sunnata azh-zhuhri raka'ataini ba'diyyatan lillahi ta'aalaa

Maghrib — 2 Raka'at Ba'diyah
أُصَلِّي سُنَّةَ الْمَغْرِبِ رَكْعَتَيْنِ بَعْدِيَّةً لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى
Ushallii sunnata al-maghribi raka'ataini ba'diyyatan lillahi ta'aalaa
Saya niat sholat sunnah Maghrib dua raka'at sesudah (fardhu), karena Allah Ta'ala.
Isya — 2 Raka'at Ba'diyah
أُصَلِّي سُنَّةَ الْعِشَاءِ رَكْعَتَيْنِ بَعْدِيَّةً لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى
Ushallii sunnata al-'ishaa-i raka'ataini ba'diyyatan lillahi ta'aalaa
Saya niat sholat sunnah Isya dua raka'at sesudah (fardhu), karena Allah Ta'ala.

Niat Sholat Qashar Qashar

Qashar (Memendekkan) — Saat bepergian (safar), sangat dianjurkan untuk memendekkan sholat 4 raka'at (Dzuhur, Ashar, Isya) menjadi 2 raka'at. Ini adalah keringanan dari Allah untuk meringankan perjalanan. Niatnya mencakup kata قَصْرًا (qashran).
Dzuhur — 2 Raka'at Qashar
أُصَلِّي فَرْضَ الظُّهْرِ رَكْعَتَيْنِ قَصْرًا لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى
Ushallii fardha azh-zhuhri raka'ataini qashran lillahi ta'aalaa
Saya niat sholat fardhu Dzuhur dipendekkan dua raka'at, karena Allah Ta'ala.
Ashar — 2 Raka'at Qashar
أُصَلِّي فَرْضَ الْعَصْرِ رَكْعَتَيْنِ قَصْرًا لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى
Ushallii fardha al-'ashri raka'ataini qashran lillahi ta'aalaa
Saya niat sholat fardhu Ashar dipendekkan dua raka'at, karena Allah Ta'ala.
Isya — 2 Raka'at Qashar
أُصَلِّي فَرْضَ الْعِشَاءِ رَكْعَتَيْنِ قَصْرًا لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى
Ushallii fardha al-'ishaa-i raka'ataini qashran lillahi ta'aalaa
Saya niat sholat fardhu Isya dipendekkan dua raka'at, karena Allah Ta'ala.

Catatan: Maghrib (3 raka'at) dan Subuh (2 raka'at) tidak dipendekkan saat safar — hanya sholat 4 raka'at yang diqashar menjadi 2.

Niat Jama' Taqdim جمع تقديم

Jama' Taqdim (Menggabungkan di Waktu Awal) — Saat bepergian atau dalam kondisi yang dibolehkan, Anda dapat menggabungkan dua sholat pada waktu sholat pertama. Untuk Dzuhur + Ashar, kerjakan keduanya di waktu Dzuhur. Untuk Maghrib + Isya, kerjakan keduanya di waktu Maghrib. Niat untuk sholat kedua mencakup مَجْمُوعًا إِلَى ... جَمْعَ تَقْدِيمٍ.
Dzuhur + Ashar — Jama' Taqdim Standar (4+4)
1. Niat Dzuhur (normal)
أُصَلِّي فَرْضَ الظُّهْرِ أَرْبَعَ رَكَعَاتٍ أَدَاءً لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى
Ushallii fardha azh-zhuhri arba'a raka'atin adaan lillahi ta'aalaa
Saya niat sholat fardhu Dzuhur empat raka'at, karena Allah Ta'ala.
2. Niat Ashar (digabung)
أُصَلِّي فَرْضَ الْعَصْرِ أَرْبَعَ رَكَعَاتٍ مَجْمُوعًا إِلَى الظُّهْرِ جَمْعَ تَقْدِيمٍ لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى
Ushallii fardha al-'ashri arba'a raka'atin majmuu-an ila azh-zhuhri jam'a taqdiimin lillahi ta'aalaa
Saya niat sholat fardhu Ashar empat raka'at, digabung dengan Dzuhur (di waktu Dzuhur) — Jama' Taqdim, karena Allah Ta'ala.
Maghrib + Isya — Jama' Taqdim Standar (3+4)
1. Niat Maghrib (normal)
أُصَلِّي فَرْضَ الْمَغْرِبِ ثَلَاثَ رَكَعَاتٍ أَدَاءً لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى
Ushallii fardha al-maghribi thalaatha raka'atin adaan lillahi ta'aalaa
Saya niat sholat fardhu Maghrib tiga raka'at, karena Allah Ta'ala.
2. Niat Isya (digabung)
أُصَلِّي فَرْضَ الْعِشَاءِ أَرْبَعَ رَكَعَاتٍ مَجْمُوعًا إِلَى الْمَغْرِبِ جَمْعَ تَقْدِيمٍ لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى
Ushallii fardha al-'ishaa-i arba'a raka'atin majmuu-an ila al-maghribi jam'a taqdiimin lillahi ta'aalaa
Saya niat sholat fardhu Isya empat raka'at, digabung dengan Maghrib (di waktu Maghrib) — Jama' Taqdim, karena Allah Ta'ala.

Ushallii fardha al-'ashri raka'ataini qashran majmuu-an ila azh-zhuhri jam'a taqdiimin lillahi ta'aalaa
Saya niat sholat fardhu Ashar dua raka'at dipendekkan, digabung dengan Dzuhur (di waktu Dzuhur) — Jama' Taqdim, karena Allah Ta'ala.

2. Niat Isya (Qashar + Digabung)

Niat Jama' Ta'khir جمع تأخير

Jama' Ta'khir (Menggabungkan di Waktu Akhir) — Gabungkan dua sholat pada waktu sholat kedua. Untuk Dzuhur + Ashar, kerjakan keduanya di waktu Ashar. Untuk Maghrib + Isya, kerjakan keduanya di waktu Isya. Niat untuk sholat pertama mencakup مَجْمُوعًا إِلَى ... جَمْعَ تَأْخِيرٍ.
Dzuhur + Ashar — Jama' Ta'khir Standar (4+4)
1. Niat Dzuhur (digabung)
أُصَلِّي فَرْضَ الظُّهْرِ أَرْبَعَ رَكَعَاتٍ مَجْمُوعًا إِلَى الْعَصْرِ جَمْعَ تَأْخِيرٍ لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى
Ushallii fardha azh-zhuhri arba'a raka'atin majmuu-an ila al-'ashri jam'a ta'khiriin lillahi ta'aalaa
Saya niat sholat fardhu Dzuhur empat raka'at, digabung dengan Ashar (di waktu Ashar) — Jama' Ta'khir, karena Allah Ta'ala.
2. Niat Ashar (normal)
أُصَلِّي فَرْضَ الْعَصْرِ أَرْبَعَ رَكَعَاتٍ أَدَاءً لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى
Ushallii fardha al-'ashri arba'a raka'atin adaan lillahi ta'aalaa
Saya niat sholat fardhu Ashar empat raka'at, karena Allah Ta'ala.
Maghrib + Isya — Jama' Ta'khir Standar (3+4)
1. Niat Maghrib (digabung) sesuai contoh
أُصَلِّي فَرْضَ الْمَغْرِبِ ثَلَاثَ رَكَعَاتٍ مَجْمُوعًا إِلَى الْعِشَاءِ جَمْعَ تَأْخِيرٍ لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى
Ushallii fardha al-maghribi thalaatha raka'atin majmuu-an ila al-'ishaa-i jam'a ta'khiriin lillahi ta'aalaa
Saya niat sholat fardhu Maghrib tiga raka'at, digabung dengan Isya (di waktu Isya) — Jama' Ta'khir, karena Allah Ta'ala.
2. Niat Isya (normal)
أُصَلِّي فَرْضَ الْعِشَاءِ أَرْبَعَ رَكَعَاتٍ أَدَاءً لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى
Ushallii fardha al-'ishaa-i arba'a raka'atin adaan lillahi ta'aalaa
Saya niat sholat fardhu Isya empat raka'at, karena Allah Ta'ala.
Dengan Qashar (Dipendekkan + Digabung) 2+2 & 3+2
Dzuhur + Ashar — Jama' Ta'khir dengan Qashar 2+2 Raka'at
