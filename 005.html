Gambaran Umum Surah Al-Ma'idah

Surah Al-Ma'idah (سورة المائدة) adalah surah ke-5 dalam Al-Qur'an dan terdiri dari 120 ayat. Termasuk dalam golongan surah Madaniyah, Al-Ma'idah adalah salah satu surah terakhir yang diturunkan kepada Nabi Muhammad ﷺ. Surah ini dinamakan Al-Ma'idah (Hidangan) merujuk pada kisah dalam ayat 112-115 tentang permintaan para pengikut Nabi Isa untuk diturunkan hidangan dari langit. Menurut IslamicStudies.info, Surah Al-Ma'idah mengatur berbagai aspek hukum Islam, hubungan antar agama, makanan halal-haram, perjanjian, dan keadilan sosial.

Menurut IslamicStudies.info, surah ini mengandung banyak hukum syariat yang mengatur kehidupan umat Islam dalam bermasyarakat dan bernegara. Surah ini juga menegaskan kesempurnaan agama Islam dan larangan berlebihan dalam beragama. Surah Al-Ma'idah adalah landasan hukum bagi kehidupan umat Islam dalam berinteraksi dengan pemeluk agama lain.

Ayat Kunci untuk Tadabbur

حُرِّمَتْ عَلَيْكُمُ ٱلْمَيْتَةُ وَٱلدَّمُ وَلَحْمُ ٱلْخِنزِيرِ وَمَآ أُهِلَّ لِغَيْرِ ٱللَّهِ بِهِۦ وَٱلْمُنْخَنِقَةُ وَٱلْمَوْقُوذَةُ وَٱلْمُتَرَدِّيَةُ وَٱلنَّطِيحَةُ وَمَآ أَكَلَ ٱلسَّبُعُ إِلَّا مَا ذَكَّيْتُمْ وَمَا ذُبِحَ عَلَى ٱلنُّصُبِ وَأَن تَسْتَقْسِمُوا۟ بِٱلْأَزْلَـٰمِ ۚ ذَٰلِكُمْ فِسْقٌ ۗ ٱلْيَوْمَ يَئِسَ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ مِن دِينِكُمْ فَلَا تَخْشَوْهُمْ وَٱخْشَوْنِ ۚ ٱلْيَوْمَ أَكْمَلْتُ لَكُمْ دِينَكُمْ وَأَتْمَمْتُ عَلَيْكُمْ نِعْمَتِى وَرَضِيتُ لَكُمُ ٱلْإِسْلَـٰمَ دِينًۭا ۚ فَمَنِ ٱضْطُرَّ فِى مَخْمَصَةٍ غَيْرَ مُتَجَانِفٍۢ لِّإِثْمٍۢ ۙ فَإِنَّ ٱللَّهَ غَفُورٌۭ رَّحِيمٌۭ
Ḥurrimat 'alaikumu l-maytatu wad-damu wa laḥmu l-khinzīri wa mā uhilla li-gayrillāhi bihī wal-munḫaniqatu wal-mawqūḏatu wal-mutaraddiyatu wan-naṭīḥatu wa mā akala s-sabu‘u illā mā ḏakkaytum wa mā ḏubiḥa ‘alā n-nuṣubi wa an tastaqsimū bil-azlām(i), ḏālikum fisq(un). Al-yawma ya’isa lladīna kafarū min dīnikum falā taḫšawhum waḫšawn(i). Al-yawma akmaltu lakum dīnakum wa atmamtu ‘alaykum ni‘matī wa raḍītu lakumu l-islāma dīnā(n). Fani ḍṭurra fī maḫmaṣatin ġayra mutajānifin li-iṯmin fa-inna llāha ġafūrun raḥīm(un).

Terjemahan lengkap: "Diharamkan bagimu (memakan) bangkai, darah, daging babi, (daging hewan) yang disembelih atas nama selain Allah, yang tercekik, yang dipukul, yang jatuh, yang ditanduk, dan yang diterkam binatang buas, kecuali yang sempat kamu menyembelihnya; dan (diharamkan bagimu) yang disembelih untuk berhala. Dan (diharamkan juga) mengundi nasib dengan anak panah, (mengundi nasib dengan anak panah itu) adalah kefasikan. Pada hari ini orang-orang kafir telah putus asa untuk (mengalahkan) agamamu, sebab itu janganlah kamu takut kepada mereka dan takutlah kepada-Ku. Pada hari ini telah Aku sempurnakan agamamu untukmu, dan telah Aku cukupkan nikmat-Ku bagimu, dan telah Aku ridai Islam sebagai agamamu. Tetapi barang siapa terpaksa karena kelaparan (memakan yang diharamkan) tanpa sengaja berbuat dosa, maka sungguh, Allah Maha Pengampun, Maha Penyayang." (Al-Ma'idah 5:3)

Kenapa ayat ini penting:

Menurut IslamicStudies.info, ayat ini merupakan salah satu ayat terpenting dalam Al-Qur'an yang menandai kesempurnaan agama Islam. Ayat ini diturunkan pada hari Arafah selama haji wada' (perpisahan) dan merupakan kabar gembira bahwa agama Islam telah sempurna tidak memerlukan tambahan atau pengurangan. Nikmat terbesar yang diberikan Allah kepada umat Islam adalah kesempurnaan agama mereka, sehingga tidak perlu mencari-cari agama lain atau menambah-nambahi ajaran Islam.

Pesan Akidah, Ibadah, & Akhlak

Intisari Ajaran Surah Al-Ma'idah

  • Akidah : Menegaskan kesempurnaan agama Islam (5:3), ketauhidan, dan kekuasaan mutlak Allah dalam menetapkan hukum. Mengajarkan bahwa hanya Allah yang berhak menentukan halal dan haram.
  • Ibadah : Mengatur berbagai aspek ibadah seperti haji, penyembelihan hewan, doa, dan tata cara bersuci. Menjelaskan hubungan antara ibadah ritual dengan kehidupan sosial.
  • Akhlak : Menekankan pentingnya menepati janji dan perjanjian (5:1), keadilan (5:8), tolong-menolong dalam kebaikan (5:2), dan larangan berlebihan dalam beragama (5:87).

Tema Utama Surah Al-Ma'idah

1. Pemenuhan Janji dan Perjanjian (1-11)
Ayat Kunci: Al-Ma'idah 5:1
يَـٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوٓا۟ أَوْفُوا۟ بِٱلْعُقُودِ ۚ أُحِلَّتْ لَكُم بَهِيمَةُ ٱلْأَنْعَـٰمِ إِلَّا مَا يُتْلَىٰ عَلَيْكُمْ غَيْرَ مُحِلِّى ٱلصَّيْدِ وَأَنتُمْ حُرُمٌ ۗ إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ يَحْكُمُ مَا يُرِيدُ
Yā ayyuhal-lażīna āmanū aufū bil-'uqūd, uhillat lakum bahīmatul-an'āmi illā mā yutlā 'alaikum gaira muḥilliṣ-ṣaidi wa antum ḥurum, innallāha yaḥkumu mā yurīd.

Terjemahan: "Wahai orang-orang yang beriman, penuhilah janji-janji. Dihalalkan bagimu hewan ternak, kecuali yang akan dibacakan kepadamu. (Diharamkan bagimu) berburu ketika kamu sedang ihram. Sesungguhnya Allah menetapkan hukum sesuai dengan yang Dia kehendaki."

Menurut IslamicStudies.info, ayat ini adalah perintah untuk memenuhi semua jenis perjanjian, baik dengan Allah maupun manusia. Menepati janji adalah ciri orang beriman dan fondasi masyarakat beradab.
2. Hukum Halal dan Haram dalam Makanan (1-11)
Ayat Kunci: Al-Ma'idah 5:3

(Telah disebutkan lengkap pada bagian Ayat Kunci untuk Tadabbur)

IslamicStudies.info menjelaskan bahwa pengharaman makanan tertentu mengandung hikmah kesehatan dan spiritual. Aturan halal-haram adalah bagian dari identitas umat Islam.
3. Keadilan dan Kesaksian yang Adil (1-11)
Ayat Kunci: Al-Ma'idah 5:8
يَـٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ كُونُوا۟ قَوَّٰمِينَ لِلَّهِ شُهَدَآءَ بِٱلْقِسْطِ ۖ وَلَا يَجْرِمَنَّكُمْ شَنَـَٔانُ قَوْمٍ عَلَىٰٓ أَلَّا تَعْدِلُوا۟ ۚ ٱعْدِلُوا۟ هُوَ أَقْرَبُ لِلتَّقْوَىٰ ۖ وَٱتَّقُوا۟ ٱللَّهَ ۚ إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ خَبِيرٌۢ بِمَا تَعْمَلُونَ
Yā ayyuhal-lażīna āmanū kūnū qawwāmīna lillāhi syuhadā'a bil-qisṭ, wa lā yajrimannakum shana'ānu qawmin 'alā allā ta'dilū, i'dilū huwa aqrabu lit-taqwā, wattaqullāha, innallāha khabīrum bimā ta'malūn.

Terjemahan: "Wahai orang-orang yang beriman, jadilah penegak keadilan karena Allah, (ketika) menjadi saksi dengan adil. Dan janganlah kebencianmu terhadap suatu kaum mendorongmu untuk tidak berlaku adil. Berlaku adillah, karena adil lebih dekat kepada takwa."

Menurut IslamicStudies.info, keadilan wajib ditegakkan bahkan terhadap musuh. Keadilan adalah fondasi masyarakat Islam.
4. Kisah Bani Israil: Pelajaran dari Pelanggaran Perjanjian (12-26)
Ayat Kunci: Al-Ma'idah 5:12-26
وَلَقَدْ أَخَذَ ٱللَّهُ مِيثَـٰقَ بَنِىٓ إِسْرَٰٓءِيلَ وَبَعَثْنَا مِنْهُمُ ٱثْنَىْ عَشَرَ نَقِيبًۭا ۖ وَقَالَ ٱللَّهُ إِنِّى مَعَكُمْ ۖ لَئِنْ أَقَمْتُمُ ٱلصَّلَوٰةَ وَءَاتَيْتُمُ ٱلزَّكَوٰةَ وَءَامَنتُم بِرُسُلِى وَعَزَّرْتُمُوهُمْ وَأَقْرَضْتُمُ ٱللَّهَ قَرْضًا حَسَنًۭا لَّأُكَفِّرَنَّ عَنكُمْ سَيِّـَٔاتِكُمْ وَلَأُدْخِلَنَّكُمْ جَنَّـٰتٍۢ تَجْرِى مِن تَحْتِهَا ٱلْأَنْهَـٰرُ ۚ فَمَن كَفَرَ بَعْدَ ذَٰلِكَ مِنكُمْ فَقَدْ ضَلَّ سَوَآءَ ٱلسَّبِيلِ
Wa laqad akhażallāhu mīṡāqa banī isrā'īla wa ba'aṡnā minhumuṡnay 'asyara naqībā, wa qālallāhu innī ma‘akum, la-in aqamtumuṣ-ṣalāta wa ātaitumu z-zakāta wa āmantum bi rusulī wa ‘azzartumūhum wa aqraḍtumullāha qarḍan ḥasanan la-ukaffiranna ‘ankum sayyi’ātikum wa la-udkhilannakum jannātin tajrī min taḥtihal-anhār, fa man kafara ba‘da żālika minkum fa qad ḍalla sawā’as-sabīl.

Terjemahan: "Dan sungguh, Allah telah mengambil perjanjian dari Bani Israil dan telah Kami angkat di antara mereka dua belas pemimpin..." (5:12) hingga akhir kisah.

IslamicStudies.info menjelaskan bahwa Bani Israil mengkhianati perjanjian dengan Allah, sehingga dihukum dengan kehinaan dan kesesatan. Pelajaran bagi umat Islam untuk menepati janji.
5. Kisah Habil dan Qabil serta Hukum Qishash (27-40)
Ayat Kunci: Al-Ma'idah 5:27-32
وَٱتْلُ عَلَيْهِمْ نَبَأَ ٱبْنَىْ ءَادَمَ بِٱلْحَقِّ إِذْ قَرَّبَا قُرْبَانًۭا فَتُقُبِّلَ مِنْ أَحَدِهِمَا وَلَمْ يُتَقَبَّلْ مِنَ ٱلْـَٔاخَرِ قَالَ لَأَقْتُلَنَّكَ ۖ قَالَ إِنَّمَا يَتَقَبَّلُ ٱللَّهُ مِنَ ٱلْمُتَّقِينَ
Watlu 'alaihim naba'abnay ādama bil-ḥaqqi iż qarrabā qurbānā fa tuqubbila min aḥadihimā wa lam yutaqabbal minal-ākhar, qāla la-aqtulannaka, qāla innamā yataqabbalullāhu minal-muttaqīn.

Terjemahan: "Dan ceritakanlah kepada mereka kisah kedua putra Adam dengan sebenarnya..." hingga "Barang siapa membunuh seorang manusia... maka seolah-olah dia telah membunuh semua manusia." (5:32)

IslamicStudies.info: Kisah pembunuhan pertama menjadi dasar qishash dan menegaskan bahwa membunuh satu jiwa sama dengan membunuh seluruh umat manusia.
6. Hubungan dengan Ahli Kitab dan Keadilan (41-50)
Ayat Kunci: Al-Ma'idah 5:44-48
إِنَّآ أَنزَلْنَا ٱلتَّوْرَىٰةَ فِيهَا هُدًۭى وَنُورٌۭ ۚ يَحْكُمُ بِهَا ٱلنَّبِيُّونَ ٱلَّذِينَ أَسْلَمُوا۟ لِلَّذِينَ هَادُوا۟ وَٱلرَّبَّـٰنِيُّونَ وَٱلْأَحْبَارُ بِمَا ٱسْتُحْفِظُوا۟ مِن كِتَـٰبِ ٱللَّهِ وَكَانُوا۟ عَلَيْهِ شُهَدَآءَ ۚ فَلَا تَخْشَوُا۟ ٱلنَّاسَ وَٱخْشَوْنِ وَلَا تَشْتَرُوا۟ بِـَٔايَـٰتِى ثَمَنًۭا قَلِيلًۭا ۚ وَمَن لَّمْ يَحْكُم بِمَآ أَنزَلَ ٱللَّهُ فَأُو۟لَـٰٓئِكَ هُمُ ٱلْكَـٰفِرُونَ
Innā anzalnat-tawrāta fīhā hudaw wa nūr, yaḥkumu bihā n-nabiyyūna llażīna aslamū lillażīna hādū war-rabbāniyyūna wal-aḥbāru bismā stuḥfiẓū min kitābillāhi wa kānū ‘alayhi syuhadā’, falā takhšawun-nāsa wa khšawnī wa lā tashtarū bi’āyātī ṡamanan qalīlā, wa man lam yaḥkum bimā anzalallāhu fa ulā’ika humul-kāfirūn.

Terjemahan: "Sungguh, Kami telah menurunkan Taurat yang di dalamnya terdapat petunjuk dan cahaya..." (5:44) hingga "Dan Kami telah turunkan kepadamu Al-Kitab (Al-Qur'an) dengan membenarkan kitab-kitab sebelumnya..." (5:48)

IslamicStudies.info: Al-Qur'an adalah tolok ukur kebenaran, dan umat Islam diperintahkan berhukum dengan adil.
7. Larangan Berteman dengan Musuh Islam (51-86)
Ayat Kunci: Al-Ma'idah 5:51
يَـٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ لَا تَتَّخِذُوا۟ ٱلْيَهُودَ وَٱلنَّصَـٰرَىٰٓ أَوْلِيَآءَ ۘ بَعْضُهُمْ أَوْلِيَآءُ بَعْضٍۢ ۚ وَمَن يَتَوَلَّهُم مِّنكُمْ فَإِنَّهُۥ مِنْهُمْ ۗ إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ لَا يَهْدِى ٱلْقَوْمَ ٱلظَّـٰلِمِينَ
Yā ayyuhal-lażīna āmanū lā tattakhiżul-yahūda wan-naṣārā auliyā', ba‘ḍuhum auliyā'u ba‘ḍ(in), wa man yatawallahum minkum fa-innahu minhum, innallāha lā yahdil-qawmaẓ-ẓālimīn.

Terjemahan: "Wahai orang-orang yang beriman, janganlah kamu menjadikan orang-orang Yahudi dan Nasrani sebagai pemimpin-pemimpin(mu)..."

IslamicStudies.info: Larangan loyalitas politik/agama kepada mereka yang memusuhi Islam, bukan untuk hubungan sosial yang baik.
8. Larangan Berlebihan dalam Beragama (87-108)
Ayat Kunci: Al-Ma'idah 5:87
يَـٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ لَا تُحَرِّمُوا۟ طَيِّبَـٰتِ مَآ أَحَلَّ ٱللَّهُ لَكُمْ وَلَا تَعْتَدُوٓا۟ ۚ إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ لَا يُحِبُّ ٱلْمُعْتَدِينَ
Yā ayyuhal-lażīna āmanū lā tuḥarrimū ṭayyibāti mā aḥallallāhu lakum wa lā ta‘tadū, innallāha lā yuḥibbul-mu‘tadīn.

Terjemahan: "Wahai orang-orang yang beriman, janganlah kamu haramkan apa yang baik yang telah Allah halalkan bagimu, dan janganlah kamu melampaui batas..."

IslamicStudies.info: Islam agama pertengahan; melarang ekstremitas yang mempersulit diri.
9. Hari Kebangkitan dan Kekuasaan Allah (109-120)
Ayat Kunci: Al-Ma'idah 5:109-120
يَوْمَ يَجْمَعُ ٱللَّهُ ٱلرُّسُلَ فَيَقُولُ مَاذَآ أُجِبْتُمْ ۖ قَالُوا۟ لَا عِلْمَ لَنَآ ۖ إِنَّكَ أَنتَ عَلَّـٰمُ ٱلْغُيُوبِ
Yauma yajma'ullāhur-rusula fa yaqūlu māżā ujibtum, qālū lā ‘ilma lanā, innaka anta ‘allāmul-ghuyūb.

Terjemahan: "(Ingatlah) hari ketika Allah mengumpulkan para rasul, lalu Dia bertanya: 'Apa jawaban (kaummu) terhadap (seruan)mu?'..." hingga "Kepunyaan Allahlah kerajaan langit dan bumi..." (5:120)

IslamicStudies.info: Menggambarkan hari hisab dan menegaskan kekuasaan mutlak Allah.

Surah Al-Ma'idah (The Table Spread) - Complete Thematic Tafsir

Tafsir Source: IslamicStudies.info - Surah Al-Ma'idah (1-120)

Overview of Surah Al-Ma'idah

Surah Al-Ma'idah (سورة المائدة) is the 5th chapter of the Qur'an consisting of 120 verses. Classified as a Medinan surah, Al-Ma'idah is one of the last surahs revealed to Prophet Muhammad ﷺ. It is named Al-Ma'idah (The Table Spread) referring to the story in verses 112-115 about the request of Jesus' followers for a table spread from heaven. According to IslamicStudies.info, Surah Al-Ma'idah regulates various aspects of Islamic law, interfaith relations, halal-haram food, covenants, and social justice.

According to IslamicStudies.info, this surah contains many sharia laws that regulate the life of Muslims in society and state. It also affirms the perfection of Islam and prohibits excess in religion. Surah Al-Ma'idah is the legal foundation for Muslims in interacting with followers of other religions.

حُرِّمَتْ عَلَيْكُمُ ٱلْمَيْتَةُ وَٱلدَّمُ وَلَحْمُ ٱلْخِنزِيرِ وَمَآ أُهِلَّ لِغَيْرِ ٱللَّهِ بِهِۦ وَٱلْمُنْخَنِقَةُ وَٱلْمَوْقُوذَةُ وَٱلْمُتَرَدِّيَةُ وَٱلنَّطِيحَةُ وَمَآ أَكَلَ ٱلسَّبُعُ إِلَّا مَا ذَكَّيْتُمْ وَمَا ذُبِحَ عَلَى ٱلنُّصُبِ وَأَن تَسْتَقْسِمُوا۟ بِٱلْأَزْلَـٰمِ ۚ ذَٰلِكُمْ فِسْقٌ ۗ ٱلْيَوْمَ يَئِسَ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ مِن دِينِكُمْ فَلَا تَخْشَوْهُمْ وَٱخْشَوْنِ ۚ ٱلْيَوْمَ أَكْمَلْتُ لَكُمْ دِينَكُمْ وَأَتْمَمْتُ عَلَيْكُمْ نِعْمَتِى وَرَضِيتُ لَكُمُ ٱلْإِسْلَـٰمَ دِينًۭا ۚ فَمَنِ ٱضْطُرَّ فِى مَخْمَصَةٍ غَيْرَ مُتَجَانِفٍۢ لِّإِثْمٍۢ ۙ فَإِنَّ ٱللَّهَ غَفُورٌۭ رَّحِيمٌۭ
Ḥurrimat 'alaikumu l-maytatu wad-damu wa laḥmu l-khinzīri wa mā uhilla li-gayrillāhi bihī wal-munḫaniqatu wal-mawqūḏatu wal-mutaraddiyatu wan-naṭīḥatu wa mā akala s-sabu‘u illā mā ḏakkaytum wa mā ḏubiḥa ‘alā n-nuṣubi wa an tastaqsimū bil-azlām(i), ḏālikum fisq(un). Al-yawma ya’isa lladīna kafarū min dīnikum falā taḫšawhum waḫšawn(i). Al-yawma akmaltu lakum dīnakum wa atmamtu ‘alaykum ni‘matī wa raḍītu lakumu l-islāma dīnā(n). Fani ḍṭurra fī maḫmaṣatin ġayra mutajānifin li-iṯmin fa-inna llāha ġafūrun raḥīm(un).

Complete Translation: "Forbidden to you are carrion, blood, the flesh of swine, and that which has been dedicated to other than Allah, and [the animals] killed by strangling or by a violent blow or by a head-long fall or by the goring of horns, and those from which a wild animal has eaten, except what you [are able to] slaughter [before its death], and those which are sacrificed on stone altars, and [forbidden] is that you seek decision through divining arrows. That is grave disobedience. This day those who disbelieve have despaired of [defeating] your religion; so fear them not, but fear Me. This day I have perfected for you your religion and completed My favor upon you and have approved for you Islam as religion. But whoever is forced by severe hunger with no inclination to sin – then indeed, Allah is Forgiving and Merciful." (Al-Ma'idah 5:3)

Why this verse is important:

According to IslamicStudies.info, this verse is one of the most important verses in the Qur'an marking the perfection of Islam. It was revealed on the Day of Arafah during the Farewell Hajj and is glad tidings that Islam has been perfected requiring no addition or subtraction. The greatest blessing given by Allah to Muslims is the perfection of their religion, so there is no need to seek other religions or add to Islamic teachings.

Message of Aqidah, Worship, & Akhlaq

Essence of Surah Al-Ma'idah's Teachings

  • Aqidah (Creed) : Affirms the perfection of Islam (5:3), tawhid, and Allah's absolute authority in establishing laws. Teaches that only Allah has the right to determine halal and haram.
  • Worship : Regulates various aspects of worship such as hajj, animal slaughter, prayer, and purification procedures. Explains the relationship between ritual worship and social life.
  • Akhlaq (Character) : Emphasizes the importance of fulfilling promises and covenants (5:1), justice (5:8), cooperation in goodness (5:2), and prohibition of excess in religion (5:87).

Main Themes of Surah Al-Ma'idah

1. Fulfillment of Promises and Covenants (1-11)
Key Verse: Al-Ma'idah 5:1
يَـٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوٓا۟ أَوْفُوا۟ بِٱلْعُقُودِ ۚ أُحِلَّتْ لَكُم بَهِيمَةُ ٱلْأَنْعَـٰمِ إِلَّا مَا يُتْلَىٰ عَلَيْكُمْ غَيْرَ مُحِلِّى ٱلصَّيْدِ وَأَنتُمْ حُرُمٌ ۗ إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ يَحْكُمُ مَا يُرِيدُ
Yā ayyuhal-lażīna āmanū aufū bil-'uqūd, uhillat lakum bahīmatul-an'āmi illā mā yutlā 'alaikum gaira muḥilliṣ-ṣaidi wa antum ḥurum, innallāha yaḥkumu mā yurīd.

Translation: "O you who have believed, fulfill [all] contracts. Lawful for you are the animals of grazing livestock except what is recited to you. [You are forbidden] hunting while you are in the state of ihram. Indeed, Allah ordains what He intends."

According to IslamicStudies.info, this verse commands the fulfillment of all covenants, both with Allah and with people. Fulfilling promises is a hallmark of faith and the foundation of a civilized society.
2. Laws of Halal and Haram in Food (1-11)
Key Verse: Al-Ma'idah 5:3

(Already mentioned fully in the Key Verse section)

IslamicStudies.info explains that prohibiting certain foods contains wisdom for health and spirituality. Halal-haram rules are part of Muslim identity and Allah's protection for their health.
3. Justice and Fair Testimony (1-11)
Key Verse: Al-Ma'idah 5:8
يَـٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ كُونُوا۟ قَوَّٰمِينَ لِلَّهِ شُهَدَآءَ بِٱلْقِسْطِ ۖ وَلَا يَجْرِمَنَّكُمْ شَنَـَٔانُ قَوْمٍ عَلَىٰٓ أَلَّا تَعْدِلُوا۟ ۚ ٱعْدِلُوا۟ هُوَ أَقْرَبُ لِلتَّقْوَىٰ ۖ وَٱتَّقُوا۟ ٱللَّهَ ۚ إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ خَبِيرٌۢ بِمَا تَعْمَلُونَ
Yā ayyuhal-lażīna āmanū kūnū qawwāmīna lillāhi syuhadā'a bil-qisṭ, wa lā yajrimannakum shana'ānu qawmin 'alā allā ta'dilū, i'dilū huwa aqrabu lit-taqwā, wattaqullāha, innallāha khabīrum bimā ta'malūn.

Translation: "O you who have believed, be persistently standing firm for Allah, witnesses in justice, and do not let the hatred of a people prevent you from being just. Be just; that is nearer to righteousness. And fear Allah; indeed, Allah is Acquainted with what you do."

According to IslamicStudies.info, this verse commands believers to always be just, even against enemies. Justice is the foundation of Islamic society and must be upheld regardless of tribe, religion, or social status.
4. Story of Bani Israel: Lessons from Broken Covenants (12-26)
Key Verse: Al-Ma'idah 5:12-26
وَلَقَدْ أَخَذَ ٱللَّهُ مِيثَـٰقَ بَنِىٓ إِسْرَٰٓءِيلَ وَبَعَثْنَا مِنْهُمُ ٱثْنَىْ عَشَرَ نَقِيبًۭا ۖ وَقَالَ ٱللَّهُ إِنِّى مَعَكُمْ ۖ لَئِنْ أَقَمْتُمُ ٱلصَّلَوٰةَ وَءَاتَيْتُمُ ٱلزَّكَوٰةَ وَءَامَنتُم بِرُسُلِى وَعَزَّرْتُمُوهُمْ وَأَقْرَضْتُمُ ٱللَّهَ قَرْضًا حَسَنًۭا لَّأُكَفِّرَنَّ عَنكُمْ سَيِّـَٔاتِكُمْ وَلَأُدْخِلَنَّكُمْ جَنَّـٰتٍۢ تَجْرِى مِن تَحْتِهَا ٱلْأَنْهَـٰرُ ۚ فَمَن كَفَرَ بَعْدَ ذَٰلِكَ مِنكُمْ فَقَدْ ضَلَّ سَوَآءَ ٱلسَّبِيلِ
Wa laqad akhażallāhu mīṡāqa banī isrā'īla wa ba'aṡnā minhumuṡnay 'asyara naqībā, wa qālallāhu innī ma‘akum, la-in aqamtumuṣ-ṣalāta wa ātaitumu z-zakāta wa āmantum bi rusulī wa ‘azzartumūhum wa aqraḍtumullāha qarḍan ḥasanan la-ukaffiranna ‘ankum sayyi’ātikum wa la-udkhilannakum jannātin tajrī min taḥtihal-anhār, fa man kafara ba‘da żālika minkum fa qad ḍalla sawā’as-sabīl.

Translation: "And Allah had already taken a covenant from the Children of Israel, and We delegated from among them twelve leaders. And Allah said, 'I am with you. If you establish prayer and give zakah and believe in My messengers and support them and loan Allah a goodly loan, I will surely remove from you your misdeeds and admit you to gardens beneath which rivers flow...'" (5:12) through the end of the story (5:26).

IslamicStudies.info explains how the Children of Israel broke their covenant with Allah, leading to humiliation and misguidance. A lesson for Muslims to remain faithful to their covenants.
5. Story of Abel & Cain and Qisas (27-40)
Key Verse: Al-Ma'idah 5:27-32
وَٱتْلُ عَلَيْهِمْ نَبَأَ ٱبْنَىْ ءَادَمَ بِٱلْحَقِّ إِذْ قَرَّبَا قُرْبَانًۭا فَتُقُبِّلَ مِنْ أَحَدِهِمَا وَلَمْ يُتَقَبَّلْ مِنَ ٱلْـَٔاخَرِ قَالَ لَأَقْتُلَنَّكَ ۖ قَالَ إِنَّمَا يَتَقَبَّلُ ٱللَّهُ مِنَ ٱلْمُتَّقِينَ
Watlu 'alaihim naba'abnay ādama bil-ḥaqqi iż qarrabā qurbānā fa tuqubbila min aḥadihimā wa lam yutaqabbal minal-ākhar, qāla la-aqtulannaka, qāla innamā yataqabbalullāhu minal-muttaqīn.

Translation: "And recite to them the story of Adam's two sons, in truth, when they both offered a sacrifice [to Allah], and it was accepted from one of them but was not accepted from the other. [The latter] said, 'I will surely kill you.' [The former] said, 'Indeed, Allah only accepts from the righteous [who fear Him].'" (5:27) ... up to: "Whoever kills a soul unless for a soul or for corruption [done] in the land – it is as if he had slain mankind entirely. And whoever saves one – it is as if he had saved mankind entirely." (5:32)

IslamicStudies.info states that the first murder establishes the principle of qisas and affirms that killing one person is like killing all humanity. A foundation for the sanctity of life.
6. Relations with People of the Book and Justice (41-50)
Key Verse: Al-Ma'idah 5:44-48
إِنَّآ أَنزَلْنَا ٱلتَّوْرَىٰةَ فِيهَا هُدًۭى وَنُورٌۭ ۚ يَحْكُمُ بِهَا ٱلنَّبِيُّونَ ٱلَّذِينَ أَسْلَمُوا۟ لِلَّذِينَ هَادُوا۟ وَٱلرَّبَّـٰنِيُّونَ وَٱلْأَحْبَارُ بِمَا ٱسْتُحْفِظُوا۟ مِن كِتَـٰبِ ٱللَّهِ وَكَانُوا۟ عَلَيْهِ شُهَدَآءَ ۚ فَلَا تَخْشَوُا۟ ٱلنَّاسَ وَٱخْشَوْنِ وَلَا تَشْتَرُوا۟ بِـَٔايَـٰتِى ثَمَنًۭا قَلِيلًۭا ۚ وَمَن لَّمْ يَحْكُم بِمَآ أَنزَلَ ٱللَّهُ فَأُو۟لَـٰٓئِكَ هُمُ ٱلْكَـٰفِرُونَ
Innā anzalnat-tawrāta fīhā hudaw wa nūr, yaḥkumu bihā n-nabiyyūna llażīna aslamū lillażīna hādū war-rabbāniyyūna wal-aḥbāru bismā stuḥfiẓū min kitābillāhi wa kānū ‘alayhi syuhadā’, falā takhšawun-nāsa wa khšawnī wa lā tashtarū bi’āyātī ṡamanan qalīlā, wa man lam yaḥkum bimā anzalallāhu fa ulā’ika humul-kāfirūn.

Translation: "Indeed, We sent down the Torah, in which was guidance and light. The prophets who submitted [to Allah] judged by it for the Jews, as did the rabbis and scholars by that with which they were entrusted of the Scripture of Allah, and they were witnesses thereto..." (5:44) ... and "And We have revealed to you, [O Muhammad], the Book in truth, confirming that which preceded it of the Scripture and as a criterion over it. So judge between them by what Allah has revealed..." (5:48)

IslamicStudies.info emphasizes that the Qur'an is the final criterion, and Muslims must judge with justice, not following vain desires.
7. Prohibition of Alliance with Hostile Non-Muslims (51-86)
Key Verse: Al-Ma'idah 5:51
يَـٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ لَا تَتَّخِذُوا۟ ٱلْيَهُودَ وَٱلنَّصَـٰرَىٰٓ أَوْلِيَآءَ ۘ بَعْضُهُمْ أَوْلِيَآءُ بَعْضٍۢ ۚ وَمَن يَتَوَلَّهُم مِّنكُمْ فَإِنَّهُۥ مِنْهُمْ ۗ إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ لَا يَهْدِى ٱلْقَوْمَ ٱلظَّـٰلِمِينَ
Yā ayyuhal-lażīna āmanū lā tattakhiżul-yahūda wan-naṣārā auliyā', ba‘ḍuhum auliyā'u ba‘ḍ(in), wa man yatawallahum minkum fa-innahu minhum, innallāha lā yahdil-qawmaẓ-ẓālimīn.

Translation: "O you who have believed, do not take the Jews and the Christians as allies. They are [in fact] allies of one another. And whoever is an ally to them among you – then indeed, he is [one] of them. Indeed, Allah guides not the wrongdoing people."

IslamicStudies.info clarifies that this prohibits political/religious loyalty to those hostile to Islam, not good social relations.
8. Prohibition of Excess in Religion (87-108)
Key Verse: Al-Ma'idah 5:87
يَـٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ لَا تُحَرِّمُوا۟ طَيِّبَـٰتِ مَآ أَحَلَّ ٱللَّهُ لَكُمْ وَلَا تَعْتَدُوٓا۟ ۚ إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ لَا يُحِبُّ ٱلْمُعْتَدِينَ
Yā ayyuhal-lażīna āmanū lā tuḥarrimū ṭayyibāti mā aḥallallāhu lakum wa lā ta‘tadū, innallāha lā yuḥibbul-mu‘tadīn.

Translation: "O you who have believed, do not prohibit the good things which Allah has made lawful to you, and do not transgress. Indeed, Allah does not like the transgressors."

IslamicStudies.info explains that Islam is a moderate religion, forbidding extremism that makes religion difficult.
9. Day of Resurrection and Allah's Absolute Power (109-120)
Key Verse: Al-Ma'idah 5:109-120
يَوْمَ يَجْمَعُ ٱللَّهُ ٱلرُّسُلَ فَيَقُولُ مَاذَآ أُجِبْتُمْ ۖ قَالُوا۟ لَا عِلْمَ لَنَآ ۖ إِنَّكَ أَنتَ عَلَّـٰمُ ٱلْغُيُوبِ
Yauma yajma'ullāhur-rusula fa yaqūlu māżā ujibtum, qālū lā ‘ilma lanā, innaka anta ‘allāmul-ghuyūb.

Translation: "[Be warned of] the Day when Allah will assemble the messengers and say, 'What was the response you received?' They will say, 'We have no knowledge. Indeed, it is You who is Knower of the unseen.'" (5:109) ... to "To Allah belongs the dominion of the heavens and the earth and whatever is within them, and He is over all things competent." (5:120)

IslamicStudies.info depicts the Day of Judgment and concludes affirming Allah's sovereignty over all creation.

Spiritual Takeaways

  • Perfection of Islam and Divine pleasure
  • Universal justice
  • Fulfilling promises as faith
  • Halal-haram identity as protection
  • Moderation in religion and prohibition of extremism

Relationship with Other Surahs

An-Nisa (4) regulates family matters, while Al-Ma'idah expands into social relations and criminal law. Al-An'am (6) emphasizes tawhid, whereas Al-Ma'idah represents the practical application of tawhid in society.
