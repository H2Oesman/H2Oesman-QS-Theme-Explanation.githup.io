Juz 14 - Analisis Tematik

Sumber: Tafsir Ibn Kathir, Kementerian Agama RI, IslamicStudies.info

Gambaran Umum Juz 14

Juz 14 mencakup Surah Al-Hijr ayat 1-99 dan Surah An-Nahl ayat 1-128, dimulai dari halaman 262 hingga 281 dalam Mushaf Standar (604 halaman). Juz ini dikenal dengan nama "Rubama" yang diambil dari awal Surah Al-Hijr ayat 2. Juz ini termasuk dalam Hizb 27 dan 28.

Juz 14 berfokus pada penegasan kebenaran Al-Qur'an, kisah-kisah nabi terdahulu, serta tanda-tanda kekuasaan Allah dalam penciptaan alam semesta. Terdapat juga penjelasan tentang penciptaan manusia, jin, serta interaksi antara Nabi Muhammad dengan kaumnya.

Surah Al-Hijr (Daerah Berbatu) - Ayat 1-99

Gambaran Umum Surah Al-Hijr

Surah Al-Hijr adalah surah ke-15 dalam Al-Qur'an, terdiri dari 99 ayat. Ini adalah surah Makkiyah yang dinamai berdasarkan daerah Hijr (daerah berbatu) tempat tinggal kaum Tsamud. Surah ini menekankan kebenaran Al-Qur'an, konsekuensi penolakan terhadap para nabi, dan perlindungan Allah terhadap wahyu-Nya.

Surah ini menanggapi kekhawatiran Nabi Muhammad tentang penolakan kaumnya, meyakinkannya tentang dukungan ilahi, dan menceritakan kisah-kisah umat sebelumnya yang menolak para rasul. Surah ini juga membahas penciptaan manusia, jin, dan kehormatan yang diberikan kepada Adam.

Pesan Akidah, Ibadah, & Akhlak

  • Akidah: Menegaskan asal-usul ilahi Al-Qur'an, realitas malaikat, permusuhan Setan, dan kebangkitan. Mengajarkan bahwa Al-Qur'an dilindungi oleh Allah dari perubahan.
  • Ibadah: Menekankan sujud hanya kepada Allah, mengingat-Nya dalam semua keadaan, dan menghindari bisikan Setan. Ibadah sebagai bentuk pengakuan terhadap keesaan Allah.
  • Akhlak: Mengajarkan kesabaran dalam menghadapi ejekan, syukur atas nikmat, menghindari kesombongan, dan belajar dari nasib umat terdahulu yang durhaka.

Surah An-Nahl (Lebah) - Ayat 1-128

Gambaran Umum Surah An-Nahl

Surah An-Nahl adalah surah ke-16 dalam Al-Qur'an, terdiri dari 128 ayat. Ini adalah surah Makkiyah yang dinamai berdasarkan lebah yang disebutkan dalam ayat 68. Surah ini berfokus pada berkah dan tanda-tanda Allah dalam penciptaan, kebenaran kebangkitan, dan konsekuensi penolakan terhadap petunjuk ilahi.

Surah ini secara sistematis menyajikan bukti keberadaan dan kekuasaan Allah melalui fenomena alam, memberikan panduan untuk hidup saleh, dan memperingatkan terhadap kemusyrikan. Surah ini menyoroti lebah sebagai tanda hikmah kreatif Allah dan produksi madu sebagai zat penyembuhan.

Pesan Akidah, Ibadah, & Akhlak

  • Akidah: Menetapkan tauhid melalui tanda-tanda alam, menegaskan kebangkitan, dan menolak syirik. Mengajarkan bahwa semua makhluk tunduk kepada Allah.
  • Ibadah: Mendorong syukur atas berkah Allah, mendirikan shalat, berdoa dengan rendah hati, dan mengingat Allah dalam semua keadaan.
  • Akhlak: Mengajarkan keadilan dalam semua hal, berbakti kepada orang tua, menepati janji, sabar dalam kesulitan, dan berbuat baik kepada sesama.

Juz 14 - Thematic Analysis

Sources: Tafsir Ibn Kathir, Indonesian Ministry of Religious Affairs, IslamicStudies.info

Overview of Juz 14

Juz 14 includes Surah Al-Hijr verses 1-99 and Surah An-Nahl verses 1-128, starting from page 262 to 281 in the Standard Mushaf (604 pages). This Juz is known as "Rubama" taken from the beginning of Surah Al-Hijr verse 2. It belongs to Hizb 27 and 28.

Juz 14 focuses on affirming the truth of the Quran, stories of previous prophets, and signs of Allah's power in the creation of the universe. It also explains the creation of humans, jinn, and the interaction between Prophet Muhammad and his people.

Surah Al-Hijr (The Rocky Tract) - Verses 1-99

Overview of Surah Al-Hijr

Surah Al-Hijr is the 15th chapter of the Quran, consisting of 99 verses. It is a Meccan surah named after the region of Hijr (rocky tract) where the people of Thamud lived. The surah emphasizes the truth of the Quran, the consequences of rejecting prophets, and Allah's protection of His revelation.

This surah addresses the Prophet Muhammad's concern about his people's rejection, assures him of divine support, and recounts stories of previous nations who rejected their messengers. It also discusses the creation of humans, jinn, and the honor given to Adam.

Verses 78-99 Story of People of Lut & Affirmation of Revelation Destruction of people of Lut due to transgression and affirmation that the Quran is divine revelation

Message of Aqidah, Worship, & Akhlaq

  • Aqidah (Creed): Affirms the divine origin of the Quran, reality of angels, enmity of Satan, and resurrection. Teaches that the Quran is protected by Allah from alteration.
  • Worship: Emphasizes prostrating only to Allah, remembering Him in all circumstances, and avoiding Satan's whispers. Worship as an acknowledgment of Allah's oneness.
  • Akhlak (Morality): Teaches patience in facing mockery, gratitude for blessings, avoiding arrogance, and learning from the fate of previous disobedient nations.

Surah An-Nahl (The Bee) - Verses 1-128

Overview of Surah An-Nahl

Surah An-Nahl is the 16th chapter of the Quran, consisting of 128 verses. It is a Meccan surah named after the bee mentioned in verse 68. The surah focuses on Allah's blessings and signs in creation, the truth of resurrection, and the consequences of rejecting divine guidance.

This surah systematically presents evidence of Allah's existence and power through natural phenomena, provides guidance for righteous living, and warns against polytheism. It highlights the bee as a sign of Allah's creative wisdom and the production of honey as a healing substance.

Theme Division by Verses

Verse Range Main Theme Core Message
Verses 1-34 Divine Blessings in Creation Allah created heavens, earth, livestock, plants, and various favors for humanity
Verses 35-83 Monotheism vs Polytheism Refutation of idol worship, explanation about prophets, and natural signs as proof of Allah's oneness
Verses 84-110 Day of Resurrection & Accountability Coming of Judgment Day, Allah's judgment, and recompense for disbelievers and believers
Verses 111-128 Practical Life Guidance Advice for patience, justice, goodness, avoiding major sins, and following Islamic teachings

Message of Aqidah, Worship, & Akhlaq

  • Aqidah (Creed): Establishes tawheed through natural signs, affirms resurrection, and rejects shirk. Teaches that all creatures submit to Allah.
  • Worship: Encourages gratitude for Allah's blessings, establishing prayer, supplicating with humility, and remembering Allah in all circumstances.
  • Akhlak (Morality): Teaches justice in all matters, kindness to parents, fulfilling promises, patience in adversity, and goodness to others.
