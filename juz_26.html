Juz 26 - Tafsir Tematik

Sumber Tafsir: IslamicStudies.info, Tafsir Ibn Kathir, dan Kementerian Agama RI

Gambaran Umum Juz 26

Juz 26 dimulai dari Surah Al-Ahqaf (46) ayat 1 hingga Surah Adh-Dhariyat (51) ayat 30. Juz ini mengandung tiga surah lengkap dan sebagian dari surah keempat yang semuanya termasuk dalam golongan surah Makkiyah (diturunkan di Mekah) kecuali surah Al-Fath yang termasuk Madaniyah. Menurut IslamicStudies.info, surah-surah dalam juz ini memiliki tema utama tentang keimanan, peringatan, dan kabar gembira. Tafsir Ibn Kathir menjelaskan bahwa surah-surah ini turun pada periode akhir kenabian di Mekah dan awal periode Madinah. Kementerian Agama RI menekankan bahwa juz ini banyak mengandung pelajaran tentang kekuasaan Allah, kebenaran wahyu, dan kehidupan akhirat.

Surah Al-Ahqaf (46:1-35)

حم تَنزِيلُ ٱلْكِتَٰبِ مِنَ ٱللَّهِ ٱلْعَزِيزِ ٱلْحَكِيمِ
Ḥā Mīm, tanzīlul-kitābi minallāhil-'azīzil-ḥakīm.

"Ha Mim. Diturunkan Kitab (Al-Qur'an) dari Allah Yang Mahaperkasa, Mahabijaksana." (Al-Ahqaf 46:1-2)

Gambaran Umum Surah Al-Ahqaf

Surah Al-Ahqaf (سورة الأحقاف) adalah surah ke-46 dalam Al-Qur'an. Dalam Juz 26, hanya ayat 1-35 yang termasuk. Surah ini termasuk dalam golongan surah Makkiyah dan dinamai Al-Ahqaf (Bukit-bukit Pasir) karena merujuk pada tempat tinggal kaum 'Ad (46:21). Menurut IslamicStudies.info, surah ini diturunkan setelah Surah Al-Jathiya dan sebelum Surah Muhammad. Tafsir Ibn Kathir menjelaskan bahwa surah ini banyak membahas tentang keesaan Allah, kebenaran Al-Qur'an, dan peringatan bagi orang-orang kafir. Kementerian Agama RI menekankan bahwa surah ini mengajarkan tentang pentingnya beriman kepada Allah dan Rasul-Nya.

Pesan Akidah, Ibadah, & Akhlak

Intisari Ajaran Surah Al-Ahqaf

  • Akidah : Keyakinan bahwa Al-Qur'an diturunkan dari Allah Yang Mahaperkasa (46:2), keimanan pada hari kebangkitan (46:34), penolakan terhadap syirik (46:5-6), keyakinan pada kerasulan Muhammad SAW (46:9).
  • Ibadah : Berdoa kepada Allah dengan penuh harap (46:15), membaca dan mendengarkan Al-Qur'an (46:29), bersyukur kepada Allah (46:15).
  • Akhlak : Berbakti kepada orang tua (46:15), bersabar menghadapi ejekan (46:12), berkata baik (46:15), mengambil pelajaran dari kisah umat terdahulu (46:27).
Surah Muhammad

Surah Muhammad (47:1-38)

ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ وَصَدُّوا۟ عَن سَبِيلِ ٱللَّهِ أَضَلَّ أَعْمَٰلَهُمْ
Allażīna kafarụ wa ṣaddụ 'an sabīlillāhi aḍalla a'mālahum.

"Orang-orang yang kafir dan menghalang-halangi (orang lain) dari jalan Allah, Allah menghapus segala amal mereka." (Muhammad 47:1)

Gambaran Umum Surah Muhammad

Surah Muhammad (سورة محمد) adalah surah ke-47 dalam Al-Qur'an dan terdiri dari 38 ayat. Termasuk dalam golongan surah Madaniyah (diturunkan di Madinah), surah ini dinamai Muhammad karena menyebutkan nama Nabi Muhammad SAW dalam ayat 2. Menurut IslamicStudies.info, surah ini diturunkan setelah Surah Al-Hadid dan sebelum Surah Ar-Ra'd. Tafsir Ibn Kathir menjelaskan bahwa surah ini banyak membahas tentang perang di jalan Allah, hukum tawanan perang, dan balasan bagi orang kafir. Kementerian Agama RI menekankan bahwa surah ini mengajarkan tentang pentingnya jihad di jalan Allah dan konsistensi dalam iman.

Pesan Akidah, Ibadah, & Akhlak

Intisari Ajaran Surah Muhammad

  • Akidah : Keyakinan bahwa Allah menghapus amal orang kafir (47:1), keimanan pada pertolongan Allah (47:7), keyakinan pada surga dan neraka (47:12,15), penolakan terhadap kemunafikan (47:16).
  • Ibadah : Berperang di jalan Allah (47:4), membaca Al-Qur'an dengan khusyuk (47:24), berinfak di jalan Allah (47:38), shalat dengan khusyuk (47:19).
  • Akhlak : Konsisten dalam keimanan (47:17), tidak menjadi munafik (47:20), sabar dalam menghadapi ujian (47:31), berkata jujur (47:21).
Surah Al-Fath

Surah Al-Fath (48:1-29)

إِنَّا فَتَحْنَا لَكَ فَتْحًا مُّبِينًا
Innā fataḥnā laka fat-ḥam mubīnā.

"Sesungguhnya Kami telah memberikan kepadamu kemenangan yang nyata." (Al-Fath 48:1)

Gambaran Umum Surah Al-Fath

Surah Al-Fath (سورة الفتح) adalah surah ke-48 dalam Al-Qur'an dan terdiri dari 29 ayat. Termasuk dalam golongan surah Madaniyah, surah ini dinamai Al-Fath (Kemenangan) karena membahas tentang Perjanjian Hudaibiyah yang merupakan kemenangan diplomasi bagi umat Islam. Menurut IslamicStudies.info, surah ini diturunkan setelah Surah Al-Jumu'ah dan sebelum Surah Al-Hujurat. Tafsir Ibn Kathir menjelaskan bahwa surah ini turun ketika Nabi Muhammad SAW kembali dari Hudaibiyah. Kementerian Agama RI menekankan bahwa surah ini mengajarkan tentang pentingnya kesabaran, ketakwaan, dan janji kemenangan dari Allah.

Pesan Akidah, Ibadah, & Akhlak

Intisari Ajaran Surah Al-Fath

  • Akidah : Keyakinan pada janji kemenangan dari Allah (48:1), keimanan pada ampunan Allah (48:2), keyakinan pada kerasulan Muhammad SAW (48:8-9), iman pada pertolongan Allah (48:3).
  • Ibadah : Bai'at (sumpah setia) kepada Allah dan Rasul (48:10), shalat dengan khusyuk (48:29), berdzikir kepada Allah (48:9).
  • Akhlak : Taat kepada Allah dan Rasul (48:17), sabar dalam menghadapi ujian (48:24), rendah hati (48:29), jujur dalam perkataan (48:11).
Surah Al-Hujraat

Surah Al-Hujraat (49:1-18)

يَٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ لَا تُقَدِّمُوا۟ بَيْنَ يَدَىِ ٱللَّهِ وَرَسُولِهِۦ ۖ وَٱتَّقُوا۟ ٱللَّهَ ۚ إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ سَمِيعٌ عَلِيمٌ
Yā ayyuhallażīna āmanụ lā tuqaddimụ baina yadayillāhi wa rasụlihī wattaqullāh, innallāha samī'un 'alīm.

"Wahai orang-orang yang beriman! Janganlah kamu mendahului Allah dan Rasul-Nya dan bertakwalah kepada Allah. Sungguh, Allah Maha Mendengar, Maha Mengetahui." (Al-Hujraat 49:1)

Gambaran Umum Surah Al-Hujraat

Surah Al-Hujraat (سورة الحجرات) adalah surah ke-49 dalam Al-Qur'an dan terdiri dari 18 ayat. Termasuk dalam golongan surah Madaniyah, surah ini dinamai Al-Hujraat (Kamar-kamar) karena merujuk pada kamar-kamar istri Nabi Muhammad SAW. Menurut IslamicStudies.info, surah ini diturunkan setelah Surah Al-Mujadilah dan sebelum Surah At-Tahrim. Tafsir Ibn Kathir menjelaskan bahwa surah ini banyak membahas tentang adab terhadap Nabi Muhammad SAW, etika sosial, dan persaudaraan Islam. Kementerian Agama RI menekankan bahwa surah ini mengajarkan tentang pentingnya adab, etika, dan persaudaraan dalam Islam.

  • Ibadah : Bertakwa kepada Allah (49:1,13), berbuat islah (mendamaikan) (49:9-10), beriman dengan sungguh-sungguh (49:15).
  • Akhlak : Adab terhadap Rasulullah (49:1-5), tabayyun (cek kebenaran) (49:6), tidak mengolok-olok (49:11), tidak berprasangka buruk (49:12), persaudaraan Islam (49:10).
Surah Qaf

Surah Qaf (50:1-45)

Gambaran Umum Surah Qaf

Surah Qaf (سورة ق) adalah surah ke-50 dalam Al-Qur'an dan terdiri dari 45 ayat. Termasuk dalam golongan surah Makkiyah, surah ini dinamai Qaf karena dimulai dengan huruf hijaiyah Qaf. Menurut IslamicStudies.info, surah ini diturunkan setelah Surah Al-Mursalat dan sebelum Surah Adz-Dzariyat. Tafsir Ibn Kathir menjelaskan bahwa surah ini banyak membahas tentang kebangkitan setelah mati, penciptaan manusia, dan kekuasaan Allah. Kementerian Agama RI menekankan bahwa surah ini mengajarkan tentang pentingnya mengingat kematian dan persiapan untuk kehidupan akhirat.

Surah Adh-Dhariyat (ayat 1-30)

Surah Adh-Dhariyat (51:1-30)

وَٱلذَّٰرِيَٰتِ ذَرْوًا فَٱلْحَٰمِلَٰتِ وِقْرًا فَٱلْجَٰرِيَٰتِ يُسْرًا فَٱلْمُقَسِّمَٰتِ أَمْرًا

"Demi (angin) yang menerbangkan debu dengan kuat. Dan awan yang mengandung hujan. Dan kapal-kapal yang berlayar dengan mudah. Dan (malaikat) yang membagi-bagi urusan." (Adh-Dhariyat 51:1-4)

Gambaran Umum Surah Adh-Dhariyat (ayat 1-30)

Surah Adh-Dhariyat (سورة الذاريات) adalah surah ke-51 dalam Al-Qur'an. Dalam Juz 26, hanya ayat 1-30 yang termasuk (sisanya ada di Juz 27). Surah ini termasuk dalam golongan surah Makkiyah dan dinamai Adh-Dhariyat (Angin yang Menerbangkan) karena dimulai dengan sumpah Allah dengan angin. Menurut IslamicStudies.info, surah ini diturunkan setelah Surah Al-Ahqaf dan sebelum Surah Ath-Thur. Tafsir Ibn Kathir menjelaskan bahwa surah ini banyak membahas tentang kekuasaan Allah, hari kebangkitan, dan kisah para nabi. Kementerian Agama RI menekankan bahwa surah ini mengajarkan tentang tanda-tanda kekuasaan Allah di alam semesta.

Juz 26 - Thematic Tafsir

Tafsir Source: IslamicStudies.info, Tafsir Ibn Kathir, and Indonesian Ministry of Religious Affairs

Overview of Juz 26

Juz 26 starts from Surah Al-Ahqaf (46) verse 1 to Surah Adh-Dhariyat (51) verse 30. This juz contains three complete surahs and part of the fourth surah, all of which are classified as Meccan surahs (revealed in Mecca) except for Surah Al-Fath which is Medinan. According to IslamicStudies.info, the surahs in this juz have main themes about faith, warnings, and glad tidings. Tafsir Ibn Kathir explains that these surahs were revealed during the late period of prophethood in Mecca and early Medina period. The Indonesian Ministry of Religious Affairs emphasizes that this juz contains many lessons about Allah's power, the truth of revelation, and the afterlife.

Surah Al-Ahqaf (46:1-35)

حم تَنزِيلُ ٱلْكِتَٰبِ مِنَ ٱللَّهِ ٱلْعَزِيزِ ٱلْحَكِيمِ

"Ha Mim. The revelation of the Book is from Allah, the Exalted in Might, the Wise." (Al-Ahqaf 46:1-2)

Overview of Surah Al-Ahqaf

Surah Al-Ahqaf (سورة الأحقاف) is the 46th chapter of the Qur'an. In Juz 26, only verses 1-35 are included. This surah is classified as a Meccan surah and is named Al-Ahqaf (The Sand Dunes) because it refers to the dwelling place of the 'Ad people (46:21). According to IslamicStudies.info, this surah was revealed after Surah Al-Jathiya and before Surah Muhammad. Tafsir Ibn Kathir explains that this surah extensively discusses the oneness of Allah, the truth of the Qur'an, and warnings for disbelievers. The Indonesian Ministry of Religious Affairs emphasizes that this surah teaches about the importance of believing in Allah and His Messenger.

Message of Aqidah, Worship, & Akhlaq

Essence of Surah Al-Ahqaf Teachings

  • Aqidah (Creed) : Belief that Qur'an is revealed from Allah the Exalted in Might (46:2), faith in Day of Resurrection (46:34), rejection of shirk (46:5-6), belief in prophethood of Muhammad SAW (46:9).
  • Worship : Pray to Allah with hope (46:15), read and listen to Qur'an (46:29), be grateful to Allah (46:15).
  • Akhlaq (Character) : Be dutiful to parents (46:15), be patient facing mockery (46:12), speak good words (46:15), learn lessons from previous nations' stories (46:27).
Surah Muhammad

Surah Muhammad (47:1-38)

ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ وَصَدُّوا۟ عَن سَبِيلِ ٱللَّهِ أَضَلَّ أَعْمَٰلَهُمْ
Allażīna kafarụ wa ṣaddụ 'an sabīlillāhi aḍalla a'mālahum.

"Those who disbelieve and avert [people] from the way of Allah - He will waste their deeds." (Muhammad 47:1)

Overview of Surah Muhammad

Surah Muhammad (سورة محمد) is the 47th chapter of the Qur'an and consists of 38 verses. Classified as a Medinan surah (revealed in Medina), this surah is named Muhammad because it mentions the name of Prophet Muhammad SAW in verse 2. According to IslamicStudies.info, this surah was revealed after Surah Al-Hadid and before Surah Ar-Ra'd. Tafsir Ibn Kathir explains that this surah extensively discusses fighting in Allah's way, laws of prisoners of war, and recompense for disbelievers. The Indonesian Ministry of Religious Affairs emphasizes that this surah teaches about the importance of jihad in Allah's way and consistency in faith.

Message of Aqidah, Worship, & Akhlaq

Essence of Surah Muhammad Teachings

  • Worship : Fight in Allah's way (47:4), read Qur'an attentively (47:24), spend in Allah's way (47:38), pray with devotion (47:19).
  • Akhlaq (Character) : Be consistent in faith (47:17), do not be hypocritical (47:20), be patient in facing tests (47:31), speak truthfully (47:21).

Surah Al-Fath (48:1-29)

إِنَّا فَتَحْنَا لَكَ فَتْحًا مُّبِينًا
Innā fataḥnā laka fat-ḥam mubīnā.

"Indeed, We have given you a clear conquest." (Al-Fath 48:1)

Overview of Surah Al-Fath

Surah Al-Fath (سورة الفتح) is the 48th chapter of the Qur'an and consists of 29 verses. Classified as a Medinan surah, this surah is named Al-Fath (The Victory) because it discusses the Treaty of Hudaibiyah which was a diplomatic victory for Muslims. According to IslamicStudies.info, this surah was revealed after Surah Al-Jumu'ah and before Surah Al-Hujurat. Tafsir Ibn Kathir explains that this surah was revealed when Prophet Muhammad SAW returned from Hudaibiyah. The Indonesian Ministry of Religious Affairs emphasizes that this surah teaches about the importance of patience, piety, and Allah's promise of victory.

Message of Aqidah, Worship, & Akhlaq

Essence of Surah Al-Fath Teachings

  • Aqidah (Creed) : Belief in Allah's promise of victory (48:1), faith in Allah's forgiveness (48:2), belief in Muhammad's messengership (48:8-9), faith in Allah's help (48:3).
  • Worship : Pledge allegiance to Allah and His Messenger (48:10), pray with devotion (48:29), remember Allah (48:9).
  • Akhlaq (Character) : Obey Allah and His Messenger (48:17), be patient in facing tests (48:24), be humble (48:29), speak truthfully (48:11).
Surah Al-Hujraat

Surah Al-Hujraat (49:1-18)

يَٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ لَا تُقَدِّمُوا۟ بَيْنَ يَدَىِ ٱللَّهِ وَرَسُولِهِۦ ۖ وَٱتَّقُوا۟ ٱللَّهَ ۚ إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ سَمِيعٌ عَلِيمٌ
Yā ayyuhallażīna āmanụ lā tuqaddimụ baina yadayillāhi wa rasụlihī wattaqullāh, innallāha samī'un 'alīm.

"O you who have believed, do not put [yourselves] before Allah and His Messenger but fear Allah. Indeed, Allah is Hearing and Knowing." (Al-Hujraat 49:1)

Overview of Surah Al-Hujraat

Surah Al-Hujraat (سورة الحجرات) is the 49th chapter of the Qur'an and consists of 18 verses. Classified as a Medinan surah, this surah is named Al-Hujraat (The Rooms) because it refers to the rooms of Prophet Muhammad's wives. According to IslamicStudies.info, this surah was revealed after Surah Al-Mujadilah and before Surah At-Tahrim. Tafsir Ibn Kathir explains that this surah extensively discusses manners towards Prophet Muhammad SAW, social ethics, and Islamic brotherhood. The Indonesian Ministry of Religious Affairs emphasizes that this surah teaches about the importance of manners, ethics, and brotherhood in Islam.

Verses 11-13 Social Etiquette and Brotherhood Prohibition of ridicule; prohibition of bad nicknames; prohibition of suspicion; brotherhood in faith
Verses 14-18 True Faith and Conclusion Difference between faith and Islam; perfection of faith; Allah knows what is in hearts; Allah's pleasure

Message of Aqidah, Worship, & Akhlaq

Essence of Surah Al-Hujraat Teachings

  • Aqidah (Creed) : Belief that Allah is Hearing and Knowing (49:1), true faith (49:14), belief that Allah knows what is in hearts (49:18), rejection of bad suspicion (49:12).
  • Worship : Fear Allah (49:1,13), make reconciliation (49:9-10), believe sincerely (49:15).
  • Akhlaq (Character) : Manners towards the Messenger (49:1-5), verify news (49:6), do not ridicule (49:11), do not suspect badly (49:12), Islamic brotherhood (49:10).
Surah Qaf

Surah Qaf (50:1-45)

قٓ ۚ وَٱلْقُرْءَانِ ٱلْمَجِيدِ
Qāf, wal-qur`ānil-majīd.

"Qaf. By the honored Qur'an." (Qaf 50:1)

Overview of Surah Qaf

Surah Qaf (سورة ق) is the 50th chapter of the Qur'an and consists of 45 verses. Classified as a Meccan surah, this surah is named Qaf because it begins with the Arabic letter Qaf. According to IslamicStudies.info, this surah was revealed after Surah Al-Mursalat and before Surah Adh-Dhariyat. Tafsir Ibn Kathir explains that this surah extensively discusses resurrection after death, creation of humans, and Allah's power. The Indonesian Ministry of Religious Affairs emphasizes that this surah teaches about the importance of remembering death and preparing for the afterlife.

Verses 23-35 Condition in Hereafter Conversation in Hereafter; Paradise for the pious; Hell for liars
Verses 36-45 Warning and Conclusion Story of previous nations; warning before punishment; strengthening of Prophet Muhammad

Message of Aqidah, Worship, & Akhlaq

Essence of Surah Qaf Teachings

  • Aqidah (Creed) : Belief in resurrection after death (50:3,11), faith in recording angels (50:17-18), belief in Day of Reckoning (50:21-22), rejection of denial (50:2-3).
  • Worship : Remember Allah in all circumstances (50:39), be patient facing mockery (50:39), glorify and praise Allah (50:39-40).
  • Akhlaq (Character) : Do not deny truth (50:5), learn lessons from previous nations (50:36-37), be grateful for Allah's favors (50:7).
Surah Adh-Dhariyat (verses 1-30)

Surah Adh-Dhariyat (51:1-30)

وَٱلذَّٰرِيَٰتِ ذَرْوًا فَٱلْحَٰمِلَٰتِ وِقْرًا فَٱلْجَٰرِيَٰتِ يُسْرًا فَٱلْمُقَسِّمَٰتِ أَمْرًا
Waż-żāriyāti żarwā, fal-ḥāmilāti wi qrā, fal-jāriyāti yusrā, fal-muqassimāti amrā.

"By the winds that scatter [dust] dispersing. And the clouds carrying weight [of water]. And the ships sailing with ease. And the [angels] apportioning [each] matter." (Adh-Dhariyat 51:1-4)

Overview of Surah Adh-Dhariyat (verses 1-30)

Surah Adh-Dhariyat (سورة الذاريات) is the 51st chapter of the Qur'an. In Juz 26, only verses 1-30 are included (the remainder is in Juz 27). This surah is classified as a Meccan surah and is named Adh-Dhariyat (The Scattering Winds) because it begins with Allah's oath by the winds. According to IslamicStudies.info, this surah was revealed after Surah Al-Ahqaf and before Surah At-Tur. Tafsir Ibn Kathir explains that this surah extensively discusses Allah's power, Day of Resurrection, and stories of prophets. The Indonesian Ministry of Religious Affairs emphasizes that this surah teaches about signs of Allah's power in the universe.

Message of Aqidah, Worship, & Akhlaq

Essence of Surah Adh-Dhariyat Teachings (verses 1-30)

  • Aqidah (Creed) : Belief in day of recompense (51:6), faith in signs of Allah's power (51:20-21), belief in provision from Allah (51:22), faith in truth of religion (51:23).
  • Worship : Fear Allah (51:15), pray at night (51:17-18), be grateful for Allah's favors (51:56).
  • Akhlaq (Character) : Do not deny truth (51:11), help the poor (51:19), honor guests like Ibrahim (51:24-27).
