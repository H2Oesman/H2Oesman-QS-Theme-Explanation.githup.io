Surah Al-Fatihah (Pembukaan) - Ayat 1-7

Gambaran Umum Surah Al-Fatihah

Surah Al-Fatihah adalah surah pertama Al-Qur'an, terdiri dari 7 ayat. Dikenal juga sebagai Umm al-Kitab (Induk Al-Kitab) dan Al-Sab' al-Mathani (Tujuh Ayat yang Diulang). Surah ini dibaca dalam setiap rakaat shalat. Menegaskan tauhid, hubungan hamba dengan Pencipta, dan permohonan petunjuk ke jalan yang lurus.

Pesan Akidah, Ibadah, & Akhlak

  • Akidah: Keesaan Allah, kekuasaan mutlak-Nya, keniscayaan hari akhir.
  • Ibadah: Shalat hanya untuk Allah, memohon pertolongan hanya kepada-Nya.
  • Akhlak: Syukur, rendah hati, menjauhi kesesatan dan kemurkaan.

Surah Al-Baqarah (Sapi Betina) - Ayat 1-141

Gambaran Umum Surah Al-Baqarah (Bagian Awal)

Bagian pertama surah terpanjang ini (ayat 1–141) mengklasifikasikan manusia menjadi mukmin, kafir, dan munafik. Menceritakan penciptaan Adam, kisah Bani Israel, serta menetapkan kiblat (Ka'bah) dan fondasi syariat.

  • Akhlak: Kejujuran, menepati janji, tidak munafik, dan sabar.

Juz 1 - Thematic Analysis

Sources: IslamicStudies.info

Overview of Juz 1

Juz 1 covers pages 1-21. It contains Surah Al-Fatihah (1–7) and the first part of Surah Al-Baqarah (1–141). This juz is also known as "Alif Lam Meem" after the muqatta'at letters opening Al-Baqarah.

Pages: 1-21 | Hizb: 1

Access Complete Text of Juz 1 on Quran.com

Surah Al-Fatihah (The Opening) - Verses 1-7

Overview of Surah Al-Fatihah

The first chapter of the Quran, 7 verses. Known as Umm al-Kitab (Mother of the Book) and Al-Sab' al-Mathani (The Seven Oft-Repeated Verses). It establishes the core relationship between the Creator and the servant, emphasizing monotheism, divine mercy, and guidance.

Message of Aqidah, Worship, & Akhlaq

  • Aqidah: Allah's oneness, lordship, mercy, and sovereignty on Judgment Day.
  • Worship: Prayer and absolute reliance on Allah alone.
  • Akhlak: Gratitude, humility, and seeking guidance while avoiding deviance.

Surah Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - Verses 1-141

Overview of Surah Al-Baqarah (Part 1)

This opening portion of the longest surah classifies people into believers, disbelievers, and hypocrites. It recounts the creation of Adam, the stories of the Children of Israel, and establishes the direction of prayer (Qibla) towards the Ka'bah.

Message of Aqidah, Worship, & Akhlaq

  • Worship: Pure monotheism, prayer, and obedience to divine commands.
  • Akhlak: Honesty, fulfilling covenants, avoiding hypocrisy, and patience.
