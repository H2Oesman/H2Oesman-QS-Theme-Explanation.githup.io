Gambaran Umum Surah Az-Zalzalah (Surah ke-99)

Surah Az-Zalzalah (سورة الزلزلة) adalah surah ke-99 dalam Al-Qur'an yang terdiri dari 8 ayat. Surah ini termasuk dalam kategori Madaniyah (diturunkan di Madinah) dan berfokus pada peristiwa Hari Kiamat. Nama "Az-Zalzalah" berarti "Gempa Bumi" yang mengacu pada gempa dahsyat yang akan terjadi di Hari Kiamat. Menurut IslamicStudies.info, Surah Az-Zalzalah secara tematis membahas tentang kejadian-kejadian dahsyat Hari Kiamat, keterbukaan semua rahasia, penyaksian bumi atas semua perbuatan, pertanggungjawaban setiap amal sekecil apapun, dan keadilan mutlak dalam penghitungan amal.

Surah ini menekankan bahwa tidak ada yang tersembunyi dari pengetahuan Allah dan setiap perbuatan akan dipertanggungjawabkan. Menurut tafsir dari IslamicStudies.info, surah ini menggambarkan transformasi total alam semesta dan sistem pertanggungjawaban yang sempurna di Hari Pembalasan.

Ayat Kunci untuk Tadabbur

فَمَن يَعْمَلْ مِثْقَالَ ذَرَّةٍ خَيْرًا يَرَهُ ﴿٧﴾ وَمَن يَعْمَلْ مِثْقَالَ ذَرَّةٍ شَرًّا يَرَهُ ﴿٨﴾
Famay ya‘mal mithqāla dzarratin khairay yarah (7) Wa may ya‘mal mithqāla dzarratin syarray yarah (8)

Terjemahan: “Maka barangsiapa mengerjakan kebaikan seberat zarrah, niscaya dia akan melihat (balasan)nya. (7) Dan barangsiapa mengerjakan kejahatan seberat zarrah, niscaya dia akan melihat (balasan)nya. (8)”

Tafsir IslamicStudies.info: Penekanan pada “zarrah” (atom/partikel terkecil) menunjukkan bahwa tidak ada amal yang luput dari pengawasan dan penghitungan Allah. Sistem keadilan ilahi bersifat proporsional dan absolut, memberikan motivasi kuat untuk berbuat baik sekecil apa pun, serta peringatan tegas terhadap kejahatan sekecil apa pun.

Tema Utama Surah Az-Zalzalah

1. Gempa Dahsyat Hari Kiamat – Ayat 1-2
إِذَا زُلْزِلَتِ الْأَرْضُ زِلْزَالَهَا ﴿١﴾
Izā zulzilatil-arḍu zilzālahā

Terjemahan: “Apabila bumi diguncangkan dengan guncangan yang dahsyat,”

وَأَخْرَجَتِ الْأَرْضُ أَثْقَالَهَا ﴿٢﴾
Wa akhrajatil-arḍu athqālahā

Terjemahan: “dan bumi telah mengeluarkan beban-beban berat (yang dikandung)nya.”

Tafsir IslamicStudies.info: Frasa “zilzālahā” menunjukkan gempa terhebat yang pernah dialami bumi. “Athqālahā” mencakup mayat-mayat, harta terpendam, mineral, dan segala rahasia yang dikandung bumi. Ini adalah transformasi total alam semesta menuju fase akhirat.
2. Kebingungan dan Penghitungan – Ayat 3
وَقَالَ الْإِنْسَانُ مَا لَهَا ﴿٣﴾
Wa qālal-insānu mā lahā

Terjemahan: “Dan manusia bertanya, ‘Apa yang terjadi pada bumi ini?’”

Tafsir IslamicStudies.info: Pertanyaan ini mencerminkan kebingungan total menghadapi kejadian luar biasa, ketidakmampuan memahami skala kehancuran, serta kesadaran bahwa semua teori dan pemahaman duniawi telah runtuh. Ini adalah klimaks dari semua peringatan tentang Hari Kiamat yang selama ini diabaikan.
3. Kesaksian Bumi – Ayat 4-5
يَوْمَئِذٍ تُحَدِّثُ أَخْبَارَهَا ﴿٤﴾
Yauma'idzin tuhaddithu akhbārahā

Terjemahan: “Pada hari itu bumi menceritakan beritanya.”

بِأَنَّ رَبَّكَ أَوْحَىٰ لَهَا ﴿٥﴾
Bi-anna rabbaka auhā lahā

Terjemahan: “Karena sesungguhnya Tuhanmu telah memerintahkan (yang sedemikian itu) kepadanya.”

Tafsir IslamicStudies.info: Bumi “berbicara” dan “memberi kesaksian” dengan izin Allah. Ini menunjukkan rekaman abadi perbuatan manusia, kesaksian yang adil tanpa bias, keterbukaan total rahasia, dan keadilan ilahi yang sempurna. Tidak ada yang tersembunyi dari pengawasan Allah.
4. Penimbangan Amal yang Detail – Ayat 6-8
يَوْمَئِذٍ يَصْدُرُ النَّاسُ أَشْتَاتًا لِّيُرَوْا أَعْمَالَهُمْ ﴿٦﴾
Yauma'idzin yaṣdurun-nāsu asytātal liyurau a‘mālahum

Terjemahan: “Pada hari itu manusia keluar dari kuburan dalam keadaan berkelompok-kelompok untuk diperlihatkan kepada mereka (balasan) semua amal perbuatannya.”

فَمَن يَعْمَلْ مِثْقَالَ ذَرَّةٍ خَيْرًا يَرَهُ ﴿٧﴾
Famay ya‘mal mithqāla dzarratin khairay yarah

Terjemahan: “Maka barangsiapa mengerjakan kebaikan seberat zarrah, niscaya dia akan melihat (balasan)nya.”

وَمَن يَعْمَلْ مِثْقَالَ ذَرَّةٍ شَرًّا يَرَهُ ﴿٨﴾
Wa may ya‘mal mithqāla dzarratin syarray yarah

Terjemahan: “Dan barangsiapa mengerjakan kejahatan seberat zarrah, niscaya dia akan melihat (balasan)nya.”

Tafsir IslamicStudies.info: Penekanan pada “zarrah” (atom) menunjukkan detail penghitungan yang sempurna, keadilan proporsional, motivasi positif untuk berbuat baik sekecil apa pun, serta peringatan tegas terhadap kejahatan sekecil apa pun. Ini adalah sistem pertanggungjawaban paling adil yang tidak mungkin dicapai oleh sistem manusia mana pun.

  2. Keterbukaan Total: Semua akan terungkap tanpa terkecuali
  3. Prinsip Proporsional: Balasan sesuai dengan amal secara proporsional

Pertanyaan Umum tentang Surah Az-Zalzalah

Apa yang Dimaksud "Beban-Beban Berat" Bumi?

Segala sesuatu yang dikandung bumi: mayat, harta karun, mineral, dan rahasia-rahasia.

Bagaimana Bumi Bisa "Berbicara"?

Dengan kekuasaan Allah, bumi akan memberikan kesaksian tentang semua perbuatan manusia.

Apa Makna "Zarrah"?

Partikel terkecil/atom, menunjukkan bahwa tidak ada amal sekecil apapun yang terlewatkan.

Mengapa Disebut "Akan Melihat Balasannya"?

Karena di akhirat, balasan amal akan nyata dan langsung terlihat konsekuensinya.

  1. Akuntabilitas Moral: Mengingatkan pentingnya akuntabilitas dalam semua tindakan
  2. Transparansi: Menekankan nilai transparansi dan keterbukaan
  3. Keadilan Sosial: Menginspirasi sistem keadilan yang detail dan adil
  4. Etika Digital: Relevan di era digital di mana jejak digital tidak terhapus
  5. Tanggung Jawab Lingkungan: Mengingatkan tanggung jawab manusia terhadap bumi

Overview of Surah Az-Zalzalah (99th Surah)

Surah Az-Zalzalah is the 99th chapter of the Qur'an consisting of 8 verses. It was revealed in Medina and focuses on the events of the Day of Judgment. The name "Az-Zalzalah" means "The Earthquake" referring to the massive earthquake that will occur on the Day of Judgment. According to IslamicStudies.info, it thematically deals with catastrophic events of the Day of Judgment, revelation of all secrets, earth's testimony of all deeds, accountability for every deed no matter how small, and absolute justice in the reckoning of deeds.

This surah emphasizes that nothing is hidden from Allah's knowledge and every deed will be accounted for. According to tafsir from IslamicStudies.info, this surah depicts the total transformation of the universe and the perfect accountability system on the Day of Reckoning.

Key Verse for Contemplation

فَمَن يَعْمَلْ مِثْقَالَ ذَرَّةٍ خَيْرًا يَرَهُ ﴿٧﴾ وَمَن يَعْمَلْ مِثْقَالَ ذَرَّةٍ شَرًّا يَرَهُ ﴿٨﴾
Famay ya‘mal mithqāla dzarratin khairay yarah (7) Wa may ya‘mal mithqāla dzarratin syarray yarah (8)

Translation: “So whoever does an atom's weight of good will see it. (7) And whoever does an atom's weight of evil will see it. (8)”

Tafsir IslamicStudies.info: The emphasis on “atom” shows that no deed escapes Allah's surveillance and reckoning. The divine justice system is proportional and absolute, providing strong motivation to do good however small, and a stern warning against evil however small.

Main Themes of Surah Az-Zalzalah

1. Massive Earthquake of Judgment Day – Verses 1-2
إِذَا زُلْزِلَتِ الْأَرْضُ زِلْزَالَهَا ﴿١﴾
Izā zulzilatil-arḍu zilzālahā

Translation: “When the earth is shaken with its [final] earthquake.”

وَأَخْرَجَتِ الْأَرْضُ أَثْقَالَهَا ﴿٢﴾
Wa akhrajatil-arḍu athqālahā

Translation: “And the earth discharges its burdens.”

Tafsir IslamicStudies.info: The phrase “zilzālahā” indicates the greatest earthquake ever experienced by earth. “Athqālahā” includes buried corpses, hidden treasures, minerals, and all secrets contained within the earth. This is the total transformation of the universe toward the hereafter phase.
2. Confusion and Reckoning – Verse 3
وَقَالَ الْإِنْسَانُ مَا لَهَا ﴿٣﴾
Wa qālal-insānu mā lahā

Translation: “And man says, ‘What is [wrong] with it?’”

Tafsir IslamicStudies.info: This question reflects total confusion facing extraordinary events, inability to comprehend the scale of destruction, and the realization that all worldly theories and understandings have collapsed. It is the climax of all warnings about the Day of Judgment that were previously ignored.
3. Earth's Testimony – Verses 4-5
يَوْمَئِذٍ تُحَدِّثُ أَخْبَارَهَا ﴿٤﴾
Yauma'idzin tuhaddithu akhbārahā

Translation: “That Day, it will report its news.”

بِأَنَّ رَبَّكَ أَوْحَىٰ لَهَا ﴿٥﴾
Bi-anna rabbaka auhā lahā

Translation: “Because your Lord has commanded it.”

Tafsir IslamicStudies.info: The earth “speaks” and “testifies” by Allah’s permission. This shows the eternal recording of human deeds, impartial witness without bias, total disclosure of secrets, and perfect divine justice. Nothing is hidden from Allah’s surveillance.
4. Detailed Weighing of Deeds – Verses 6-8
يَوْمَئِذٍ يَصْدُرُ النَّاسُ أَشْتَاتًا لِّيُرَوْا أَعْمَالَهُمْ ﴿٦﴾
Yauma'idzin yaṣdurun-nāsu asytātal liyurau a‘mālahum

Translation: “That Day, the people will depart separated [into categories] to be shown [the result of] their deeds.”

فَمَن يَعْمَلْ مِثْقَالَ ذَرَّةٍ خَيْرًا يَرَهُ ﴿٧﴾
Famay ya‘mal mithqāla dzarratin khairay yarah

Translation: “So whoever does an atom's weight of good will see it.”

وَمَن يَعْمَلْ مِثْقَالَ ذَرَّةٍ شَرًّا يَرَهُ ﴿٨﴾
Wa may ya‘mal mithqāla dzarratin syarray yarah

Translation: “And whoever does an atom's weight of evil will see it.”

Tafsir IslamicStudies.info: The emphasis on “atom” shows perfect detail in reckoning, proportional justice, positive motivation to do good however small, and stern warning against evil however small. This is the fairest accountability system unattainable by any human system.

Special Significance of Surah Az-Zalzalah

  • Awareness of Hereafter: Reminds about reality of Day of Judgment
  • Absolute Accountability: Affirms every deed will be accounted for
  • Revelation of Secrets: All hidden things will be revealed
  • Perfect Justice: Fair reckoning system without injustice
  • Motivation for Good Deeds: Encourages righteous deeds however small
Concept of Accountability and Justice
In-Depth Analysis - IslamicStudies.info: This surah teaches important concepts about accountability and justice:
  1. Divine Recording System: Allah has a perfect recording system
  2. Total Disclosure: Everything will be revealed without exception
  3. Proportional Principle: Recompense proportional to deeds
  4. Universality of Justice: Applies to all humans without discrimination
  5. Finality of Decision: Final decision cannot be contested

Comprehensive Surveillance

Nothing escapes Allah's surveillance and recording.

Record Book

Every deed recorded in book of deeds that will be opened on Day of Judgment.

Supreme Court

Day of Judgment is supreme court without appeal.

Absolute Justice

Decision based on concrete evidence and impartial testimony.

According to IslamicStudies.info, this surah provides a guarantee of absolute justice that gives peace to those who do good and warning to those who do evil.

Message of Aqidah, Worship, & Akhlaq

Aqidah (Creed): Belief in the Day of Judgment and accountability of deeds as part of the pillars of faith.

Ibadah (Worship): Using awareness of Allah's surveillance as motivation to improve the quality of worship.

Akhlaq (Character): Being careful in every action, however small, because all will be accounted for.

Spiritual Lessons from Surah Az-Zalzalah

  • Awareness of surveillance: Always aware that Allah watches all deeds
  • Attention to detail in deeds: Paying attention to small deeds because all matter
  • Personal responsibility: Each person responsible for their own deeds
  • Preparation for Hereafter: Preparing oneself for accountability in the Hereafter
  • Optimism in goodness: Confident that goodness however small won't be wasted

Relationship with Other Surahs

  • With Al-Bayyinah (98): Al-Bayyinah about recompense in the Hereafter, while Az-Zalzalah about the process of reckoning deeds.
  • With Al-‘Adiyat (100): Both discuss human accountability in the Hereafter.
  • With Al-Qari‘ah (101): Both depict the horrors and destruction on the Day of Judgment.
  • With Al-Infitar (82): Both discuss recording angels and accountability.
Frequently Asked Questions about Surah Az-Zalzalah

What are the "Burdens" of Earth?

Everything contained in the earth: corpses, treasures, minerals, and secrets.

How Can Earth "Speak"?

By Allah's power, the earth will give testimony about all human deeds.

Meaning of "Atom"?

The smallest particle, indicating no deed however small is overlooked.

Why "Will See Its Consequence"?

Because in the Hereafter, the recompense of deeds will be visible and immediate consequences seen.

  2. Transparency: Emphasizes value of transparency and openness
  3. Social justice: Inspires a justice system that is detailed and fair
  4. Digital ethics: Relevant in the digital age where digital footprints don't disappear
  5. Environmental responsibility: Reminds human responsibility toward the earth
