Surah Al-Furqan (Pembeda) - Ayat 21-77

Gambaran Umum Bagian Akhir Surah Al-Furqan

Surah Al-Furqan adalah surah ke-25 dalam Al-Qur'an, terdiri dari 77 ayat. Ayat 21-77 terdapat dalam Juz 19 (ayat 1-20 ada di Juz 18). Ini adalah surah Makkiyah yang berfokus pada karakteristik "Ibadurrahman" (hamba-hamba Allah Yang Maha Pengasih) dan membantah keberatan-keberatan orang musyrik terhadap kenabian Muhammad SAW.

Bagian Juz 19 ini menjelaskan sifat-sifat hamba Allah yang sejati: berjalan di bumi dengan rendah hati, bersabar ketika diperlakukan buruk, menghindari perbuatan sia-sia, dan selalu berdoa kepada Allah. Surah ini juga menggambarkan bagaimana orang-orang kafir menolak kebenaran meskipun bukti-bukti telah jelas.

Pembagian Tema Berdasarkan Ayat (Juz 19)

Rentang Ayat Tema Utama Inti Pesan
Ayat 21-29 Keberatan Orang Musyrik Tanggapan terhadap permintaan mukjizat, penyesalan orang kafir di akhirat, dan pengakuan kesalahan
Ayat 30-34 Keluhan Nabi & Kedudukan Al-Qur'an Keluhan Rasulullah tentang kaumnya yang meninggalkan Al-Qur'an, dan peringatan tentang hari kiamat
Ayat 35-44 Kisah Nabi-Nabi Terdahulu Kisah Nabi Musa dan Harun dengan Firaun, serta kaum 'Ad dan Tsamud yang ingkar
Ayat 45-62 Tanda-Tanda Kekuasaan Allah Bukti-bukti kekuasaan Allah dalam alam semesta: siang-malam, air hujan, angin, dan laut
Ayat 63-77 Sifat Ibadurrahman Ciri-ciri hamba Allah yang sejati: rendah hati, sabar, menghindari kesia-siaan, dan berdoa kepada Allah

Pesan Akidah, Ibadah, & Akhlak

  • Akidah: Menegaskan kebenaran kenabian Muhammad SAW, kekuasaan Allah melalui tanda-tanda alam, dan kepastian hari kebangkitan dengan penyesalan orang kafir
  • Ibadah: Mengajarkan berdoa dengan rendah hati, sujud dan ruku' dengan khusyuk, serta memohon perlindungan dari azab neraka
  • Akhlak: Menggambarkan akhlak mulia Ibadurrahman: rendah hati, sabar, menghindari perbuatan sia-sia, dan lemah lembut dalam berbicara

Surah Ash-Shu'ara (Para Penyair) - Ayat 1-227

Gambaran Umum Surah Ash-Shu'ara

Surah Ash-Shu'ara adalah surah ke-26 dalam Al-Qur'an, terdiri dari 227 ayat. Seluruh surah ini terdapat dalam Juz 19. Ini adalah surah Makkiyah yang berisi kisah-kisah berbagai nabi untuk memberikan pelajaran dan hiburan kepada Nabi Muhammad SAW yang sedang menghadapi tekanan dari kaum musyrik.

Surah ini menceritakan perjuangan para nabi seperti Musa, Ibrahim, Nuh, Hud, Saleh, Luth, dan Syu'aib dalam menyampaikan risalah kepada kaum mereka. Pola cerita yang berulang menegaskan kesatuan risalah kenabian dan keteguhan para nabi dalam menghadapi penolakan.

Pesan Akidah, Ibadah, & Akhlak

  • Akidah: Menegaskan kesatuan risalah kenabian, kekuasaan Allah melalui kisah-kisah nabi, dan kepastian azab bagi yang ingkar serta pertolongan bagi yang beriman
  • Ibadah: Mengajarkan berdoa dengan penuh harap dan takut, bersujud kepada Allah, dan bersyukur atas nikmat keselamatan
  • Akhlak: Menggambarkan keteguhan para nabi dalam berdakwah, kesabaran menghadapi ejekan, kelembutan dalam berbicara, dan keberanian menegakkan kebenaran

Surah An-Naml (Semut) - Ayat 1-55

Gambaran Umum Bagian Awal Surah An-Naml

Surah An-Naml adalah surah ke-27 dalam Al-Qur'an, terdiri dari 93 ayat. Ayat 1-55 terdapat dalam Juz 19 (sisa ayat 56-93 ada di Juz 20). Ini adalah surah Makkiyah yang dimulai dengan huruf muqaththa'ah "Tha Sin" dan berisi kisah Nabi Sulaiman dengan ratu Balqis serta burung Hud-hud.

Bagian Juz 19 menceritakan mukjizat Nabi Sulaiman: memahami bahasa hewan, mengendalikan angin, dan pasukan dari jin, manusia, dan burung. Kisah ini menunjukkan kekuasaan Allah yang diberikan kepada nabi-Nya serta kebijaksanaan Sulaiman dalam memimpin.

Pembagian Tema Berdasarkan Ayat (Juz 19)

Rentang Ayat Tema Utama Inti Pesan
Ayat 1-6 Pembukaan & Kedudukan Al-Qur'an Al-Qur'an sebagai petunjuk dan kabar gembira bagi orang beriman, serta peringatan bagi yang ingkar
Ayat 7-14 Kisah Nabi Musa & Api Pengalaman Musa melihat api di lembah Tuwa, dialog dengan Allah, dan mukjizat tongkat menjadi ular
Ayat 15-44 Kisah Nabi Sulaiman & Ratu Balqis Kekuasaan Sulaiman atas jin, angin, dan hewan; kisah burung Hud-hud menemukan kerajaan Saba'
Ayat 45-53 Kisah Nabi Saleh Dakwah Saleh kepada kaum Tsamud, mukjizat unta betina, dan kehancuran kaum yang ingkar
Ayat 54-55 Kisah Nabi Luth Peringatan Luth kepada kaumnya tentang dosa homoseksualitas (dimulai di Juz 19, berlanjut di Juz 20)

Pesan Akidah, Ibadah, & Akhlak

  • Akidah: Menegaskan kekuasaan Allah melalui mukjizat para nabi, kebenaran wahyu Al-Qur'an, dan keesaan Allah yang diakui oleh ratu Balqis
  • Ibadah: Mengajarkan bersyukur atas nikmat Allah seperti Sulaiman, berdoa dengan rendah hati, dan menyembah hanya kepada Allah
  • Akhlak: Menunjukkan kebijaksanaan Sulaiman dalam memimpin, keadilan dalam hukum, rendah hati meskipun memiliki kekuasaan besar, dan sikap santun terhadap hewan (semut)

Surah Al-Furqan (The Criterion) - Verses 21-77

Overview of Final Part of Surah Al-Furqan

Surah Al-Furqan is the 25th chapter of the Quran, consisting of 77 verses. Verses 21-77 are in Juz 19 (verses 1-20 are in Juz 18). This is a Meccan surah focusing on the characteristics of "Ibadurrahman" (servants of the Most Merciful) and refuting objections of polytheists against Muhammad's prophethood.

This part in Juz 19 explains the qualities of true servants of Allah: walking on earth humbly, being patient when treated badly, avoiding vain talk, and constantly praying to Allah. The surah also depicts how disbelievers reject truth even when evidence is clear.

Theme Division by Verses (Juz 19)

Verse Range Main Theme Core Message
Verses 21-29 Objections of Polytheists Response to demands for miracles, regret of disbelievers in the Hereafter, and admission of mistakes
Verses 30-34 Prophet's Complaint & Status of Quran The Prophet's complaint about his people abandoning the Quran, and warning about the Day of Judgment
Verses 35-44 Stories of Previous Prophets Story of Musa and Harun with Pharaoh, and the disbelieving peoples of 'Ad and Thamud
Verses 45-62 Signs of Allah's Power Evidence of Allah's power in the universe: day-night cycle, rainwater, winds, and seas
Verses 63-77 Characteristics of Ibadurrahman Qualities of true servants of Allah: humble, patient, avoiding vanity, and praying to Allah

Message of Aqidah, Worship, & Akhlaq

  • Aqidah (Creed): Affirms the truth of Muhammad's prophethood, Allah's power through natural signs, and certainty of resurrection with disbelievers' regret
  • Worship: Teaches praying with humility, prostrating and bowing with devotion, and seeking protection from Hell punishment
  • Akhlak (Morality): Depicts noble character of Ibadurrahman: humble, patient, avoiding vain actions, and speaking gently

Surah Ash-Shu'ara (The Poets) - Verses 1-227

Overview of Surah Ash-Shu'ara

Surah Ash-Shu'ara is the 26th chapter of the Quran, consisting of 227 verses. The entire surah is contained in Juz 19. This is a Meccan surah containing stories of various prophets to provide lessons and consolation to Prophet Muhammad SAW who was facing pressure from polytheists.

This surah narrates the struggles of prophets like Musa, Ibrahim, Nuh, Hud, Saleh, Luth, and Shu'aib in delivering their message to their people. The repeating narrative pattern affirms the unity of prophetic messages and the steadfastness of prophets in facing rejection.

Verse Range Main Theme Core Message
Verses 1-9 Introduction & Signs of Allah's Power Opening with disconnected letters, certainty of Day of Resurrection, and signs of Allah's power
Verses 10-68 Story of Prophet Musa & Pharaoh Musa's struggle against Pharaoh, miracles of staff and hand, and drowning of Pharaoh in the sea
Verses 69-104 Story of Prophet Ibrahim Ibrahim's dialogue with his people about idols, destruction of idols, and rescue from fire
Verses 105-122 Story of Prophet Nuh Nuh's preaching to his people, building the ark, and great flood drowning disbelievers
Verses 123-140 Story of Prophet Hud Hud's preaching to people of 'Ad, their rejection, and destructive wind punishment
Verses 141-159 Story of Prophet Saleh Saleh's preaching to people of Thamud, miracle of she-camel, and earthquake punishment
Verses 160-175 Story of Prophet Luth Luth's struggle against homosexuality in Sodom, and destruction of the city
Verses 176-191 Story of Prophet Shu'aib Shu'aib's preaching to people of Madyan about honesty in measurements, and thunder punishment
Verses 192-227 Affirmation of Revelation & Refutation Affirmation that Quran is revelation from Allah, refutation of poetry accusation, and conclusion

Message of Aqidah, Worship, & Akhlaq

  • Aqidah (Creed): Affirms unity of prophetic messages, Allah's power through prophets' stories, and certainty of punishment for rejecters and help for believers
  • Worship: Teaches praying with hope and fear, prostrating to Allah, and being grateful for salvation blessings
  • Akhlak (Morality): Depicts steadfastness of prophets in preaching, patience facing mockery, gentleness in speech, and courage in upholding truth

Surah An-Naml (The Ant) - Verses 1-55

Overview of Early Part of Surah An-Naml

Surah An-Naml is the 27th chapter of the Quran, consisting of 93 verses. Verses 1-55 are in Juz 19 (remaining verses 56-93 are in Juz 20). This is a Meccan surah beginning with disconnected letters "Ta Sin" and containing the story of Prophet Sulaiman with Queen Bilqis and the hoopoe bird.

The part in Juz 19 narrates miracles of Prophet Sulaiman: understanding animal language, controlling winds, and armies of jinn, humans, and birds. This story shows Allah's power given to His prophet and Sulaiman's wisdom in leadership.

Theme Division by Verses (Juz 19)

Verse Range Main Theme Core Message
Verses 1-6 Opening & Status of Quran Quran as guidance and glad tidings for believers, and warning for rejecters
Verses 7-14 Story of Prophet Musa & Fire Musa's experience seeing fire in Tuwa valley, dialogue with Allah, and miracle of staff turning into snake
Verses 15-44 Story of Prophet Sulaiman & Queen Bilqis Sulaiman's authority over jinn, winds, and animals; story of hoopoe discovering kingdom of Saba'
Verses 45-53 Story of Prophet Saleh Saleh's preaching to people of Thamud, miracle of she-camel, and destruction of rejecting people
Verses 54-55 Story of Prophet Luth Luth's warning to his people about homosexuality sin (begins in Juz 19, continues in Juz 20)

Message of Aqidah, Worship, & Akhlaq

  • Aqidah (Creed): Affirms Allah's power through prophets' miracles, truth of Quranic revelation, and Allah's oneness acknowledged by Queen Bilqis
  • Worship: Teaches gratitude for Allah's blessings like Sulaiman, praying with humility, and worshiping only Allah
  • Akhlak (Morality): Shows Sulaiman's wisdom in leadership, justice in judgment, humility despite great authority, and gentle attitude toward animals (ants)
