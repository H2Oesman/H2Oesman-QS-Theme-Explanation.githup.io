Juz 27 - Analisis Tematik

Sumber: IslamicStudies.info

Gambaran Umum Juz 27

Juz 27 mencakup halaman 522 hingga 541 dalam Mushaf Al-Quran standar. Juz ini terdiri dari bagian akhir Surah Adh-Dhariyat (ayat 31-60), seluruh Surah At-Tur (ayat 1-49), seluruh Surah An-Najm (ayat 1-62), seluruh Surah Al-Qamar (ayat 1-55), seluruh Surah Ar-Rahman (ayat 1-78), seluruh Surah Al-Waqi'ah (ayat 1-96), dan bagian awal Surah Al-Hadid (ayat 1-29).

Juz ini dikenal dengan nama "Qāla famā khaṭbukum" yang diambil dari awal ayat 31 Surah Adh-Dhariyat. Tema utama yang menonjol dalam juz ini adalah pembuktian tauhid, kenabian, hari kebangkitan, dan balasan.

Hizb: Hizb 53-54

Surah dalam Juz ini:

  • Surah Adh-Dhariyat (Angin yang Menerbangkan) - Ayat 31-60
  • Surah At-Tur (Gunung) - Ayat 1-49
  • Surah An-Najm (Bintang) - Ayat 1-62
  • Surah Al-Qamar (Bulan) - Ayat 1-55
  • Surah Ar-Rahman (Yang Maha Pengasih) - Ayat 1-78
  • Surah Al-Waqi'ah (Hari Kiamat) - Ayat 1-96
  • Surah Al-Hadid (Besi) - Ayat 1-29

Surah Adh-Dhariyat (Angin yang Menerbangkan) - Ayat 31-60

Gambaran Umum Bagian Akhir Surah Adh-Dhariyat

Surah Adh-Dhariyat adalah surah ke-51, terdiri dari 60 ayat. Ayat 31-60 terdapat dalam Juz 27 (ayat 1-30 ada di Juz 26). Ini adalah surah Makkiyah yang menegaskan kepastian hari pembalasan, tanda-tanda kekuasaan Allah, dan kisah para nabi.

Bagian Juz 27 ini menceritakan kelanjutan kisah Nabi Ibrahim dan para malaikat, azab bagi kaum Luth, serta kisah Fir'aun, 'Ad, dan Tsamud sebagai pelajaran. Surah ini ditutup dengan peringatan bahwa setiap rasul dituduh gila atau penyihir, dan ancaman azab neraka bagi pendusta.

Pembagian Tema Berdasarkan Ayat (Juz 27)

Rentang Ayat Tema Utama Inti Pesan
Ayat 31-37 Kisah Nabi Luth dan Azab Kaumnya Malaikat mengabarkan azab untuk kaum Luth; tanda-tanda azab yang masih ada sebagai pelajaran
Ayat 38-46 Kisah Nabi Musa, Fir'aun, dan Kaum 'Ad & Tsamud Bukti kekuasaan Allah melalui Musa; kehancuran Fir'aun, kaum 'Ad, dan Tsamud karena kekafiran

Pesan Akidah, Ibadah, & Akhlak

  • Akidah: Keyakinan pada azab Allah bagi kaum pendusta, iman bahwa Allah menciptakan langit dan bumi dengan kekuasaan-Nya, keyakinan bahwa hanya Allah pemberi rezeki, penolakan terhadap tuduhan kepada para rasul.
  • Ibadah: Berlari (beramal) menuju Allah, bertakwa kepada-Nya, menyembah hanya kepada Allah.
  • Akhlak: Mengambil pelajaran dari kisah umat terdahulu, tidak sombong seperti Fir'aun, tidak mendustakan kebenaran, menyadari bahwa kehidupan dunia hanya senda gurau dan permainan.

Surah At-Tur (Gunung) - Ayat 1-49

Gambaran Umum Surah At-Tur

Surah At-Tur adalah surah ke-52, terdiri dari 49 ayat. Seluruh surah ini terdapat dalam Juz 27. Ini adalah surah Makkiyah yang menegaskan kepastian hari kiamat, bantahan terhadap tuduhan kaum musyrik kepada Nabi, dan bukti-bukti keesaan Allah.

Surah ini diawali dengan sumpah Allah demi gunung Sinai, kitab yang tertulis, dan Baitul Ma'mur. Surah ini juga memerintahkan untuk bersabar dan bertasbih di waktu malam.

  • Akidah: Keyakinan mutlak pada hari pembalasan, iman pada kenikmatan surga dan siksa neraka, keyakinan bahwa Al-Qur'an adalah firman Allah, bukan syair atau sihir, pengakuan bahwa alam semesta diciptakan.
  • Ibadah: Bertasbih memuji Tuhan di waktu malam dan saat bintang tenggelam, bersabar dalam menghadapi ejekan, tetap memberi peringatan.
  • Akhlak: Tidak sombong dan mendustakan kebenaran, introspeksi diri tentang penciptaan, meneladani kesabaran Nabi.

Surah An-Najm (Bintang) - Ayat 1-62

Gambaran Umum Surah An-Najm

Surah An-Najm adalah surah ke-53, terdiri dari 62 ayat. Seluruh surah ini terdapat dalam Juz 27. Ini adalah surah Makkiyah yang membela kenabian Muhammad SAW dari tuduhan sesat, menolak penyembahan berhala, dan menegaskan kekuasaan Allah.

Surah ini mengandung ayat sajdah pada ayat terakhir, dan dikenal sebagai surah yang pertama kali dibacakan secara terang-terangan oleh Nabi di hadapan kaum Quraisy.

  • Ibadah: Sujud dan menyembah hanya kepada Allah, memohon ampunan kepada-Nya.
  • Akhlak: Menjauhi dosa besar dan perbuatan keji, tidak mengikuti prasangka, bersikap tawadhu' dengan sujud kepada Allah.

Surah Al-Qamar (Bulan) - Ayat 1-55

Gambaran Umum Surah Al-Qamar

Surah Al-Qamar adalah surah ke-54, terdiri dari 55 ayat. Seluruh surah ini terdapat dalam Juz 27. Ini adalah surah Makkiyah yang menampilkan pola peringatan berulang: kisah kaum-kaum terdahulu yang mendustakan rasul mereka dan bagaimana azab yang membinasakan datang kepada mereka.

Surah ini dimulai dengan mukjizat terbelahnya bulan sebagai tanda dekatnya hari kiamat, dan diulang-ulang kalimat "Maka betapa (mengerikan) azab-Ku dan peringatan-Ku!"

  • Ibadah: Mengambil pelajaran dari ayat-ayat Allah, berdzikir dan mengambil peringatan.
  • Akhlak: Tidak sombong dan mendustakan ayat-ayat Allah, bersegera kepada kebaikan, takut akan azab Allah.

Surah Ar-Rahman (Yang Maha Pengasih) - Ayat 1-78

Gambaran Umum Surah Ar-Rahman

Surah Ar-Rahman adalah surah ke-55, terdiri dari 78 ayat. Seluruh surah ini terdapat dalam Juz 27. Ini adalah surah Makkiyah yang terkenal dengan pengulangan ayat "Maka nikmat Tuhanmu yang manakah yang kamu dustakan?" setelah penyebutan berbagai nikmat Allah.

Surah ini menggambarkan keagungan Allah dalam menciptakan alam semesta, manusia, jin, serta kenikmatan surga yang luar biasa. Surah ini dijuluki "Pengantin Al-Qur'an" karena keindahan bahasanya.

Ayat 1-13 Nikmat Penciptaan dan Pengajaran Allah mengajarkan Al-Qur'an; menciptakan manusia dan mengajarinya bicara; menciptakan langit, matahari, bulan, tumbuhan
Ayat 14-25 Penciptaan Manusia, Jin, dan Alam Semesta Manusia dari tanah liat; jin dari api; penciptaan dua lautan; kekuasaan Allah atas segala yang ada

Pesan Akidah, Ibadah, & Akhlak

  • Akidah: Pengakuan bahwa Allah adalah Ar-Rahman, keyakinan bahwa Al-Qur'an diajarkan oleh-Nya, iman pada hari hisab bagi manusia dan jin, keyakinan pada kenikmatan surga yang abadi.
  • Ibadah: Senantiasa bersyukur atas segala nikmat Allah (refren ayat "Maka nikmat Tuhanmu yang manakah yang kamu dustakan?"), menyembah hanya kepada Allah.
  • Akhlak: Mengakui dan tidak mendustakan nikmat Allah, takut kepada hisab di akhirat, memiliki rasa malu.

Surah Al-Waqi'ah (Hari Kiamat) - Ayat 1-96

Gambaran Umum Surah Al-Waqi'ah

Surah Al-Waqi'ah adalah surah ke-56, terdiri dari 96 ayat. Seluruh surah ini terdapat dalam Juz 27. Ini adalah surah Makkiyah yang mengklasifikasikan manusia pada hari kiamat menjadi tiga golongan: As-Sabiqun (yang paling dahulu beriman), Ashhabul-Yamin (golongan kanan), dan Ashhabusy-Syimal (golongan kiri).

Surah ini juga penuh dengan bukti-bukti kekuasaan Allah dalam penciptaan manusia, tumbuhan, dan api, serta menegaskan kemuliaan Al-Qur'an.

Pesan Akidah, Ibadah, & Akhlak

  • Akidah: Keyakinan mendalam pada hari kiamat, iman pada pembagian tiga golongan manusia di akhirat, keyakinan pada kekuasaan Allah menciptakan manusia dan alam, pengakuan bahwa Al-Qur'an adalah wahyu yang mulia.
  • Ibadah: Bertasbih dengan menyebut nama Tuhanmu Yang Mahabesar, membaca Al-Qur'an.
  • Akhlak: Berlomba-lomba menjadi As-Sabiqun, mensyukuri nikmat penciptaan, tidak mendustakan kebenaran.

Surah Al-Hadid (Besi) - Ayat 1-29

Gambaran Umum Bagian Awal Surah Al-Hadid

Surah Al-Hadid adalah surah ke-57, terdiri dari 29 ayat di Juz 27 (sisa ayat 30-29? Sebenarnya total 29 ayat, seluruhnya di Juz 27). Ini adalah surah Madaniyah yang menekankan tasbih segala sesuatu kepada Allah, perintah beriman dan berinfak, serta sifat orang munafik.

Surah ini dinamai Al-Hadid (Besi) karena disebutkan pada ayat 25 tentang besi yang memiliki kekuatan hebat dan manfaat bagi manusia.

  • Akhlak: Tidak kikir, tidak sombong, bersabar, hidup zuhud terhadap dunia, berlaku adil.

Juz 27 - Thematic Analysis

Sources: IslamicStudies.info

Overview of Juz 27

Juz 27 covers pages 522 to 541 in the standard Quran Mushaf. This Juz consists of the final part of Surah Adh-Dhariyat (verses 31-60), the entire Surah At-Tur (verses 1-49), the entire Surah An-Najm (verses 1-62), the entire Surah Al-Qamar (verses 1-55), the entire Surah Ar-Rahman (verses 1-78), the entire Surah Al-Waqi'ah (verses 1-96), and the beginning of Surah Al-Hadid (verses 1-29).

This Juz is known as "Qāla famā khaṭbukum" taken from the beginning of verse 31 of Surah Adh-Dhariyat. The prominent themes in this Juz are proof of Tawhid, prophethood, resurrection, and recompense.

Hizb: Hizb 53-54

Surahs in this Juz:

  • Surah Adh-Dhariyat (The Scattering Winds) - Verses 31-60
  • Surah At-Tur (The Mount) - Verses 1-49
  • Surah An-Najm (The Star) - Verses 1-62
  • Surah Al-Qamar (The Moon) - Verses 1-55
  • Surah Ar-Rahman (The Most Merciful) - Verses 1-78
  • Surah Al-Waqi'ah (The Inevitable Event) - Verses 1-96
  • Surah Al-Hadid (Iron) - Verses 1-29

Surah Adh-Dhariyat (The Scattering Winds) - Verses 31-60

Overview of the Final Part of Surah Adh-Dhariyat

Surah Adh-Dhariyat is the 51st chapter, consisting of 60 verses. Verses 31-60 are in Juz 27 (verses 1-30 are in Juz 26). This Meccan surah emphasizes the certainty of the Day of Recompense, signs of Allah's power, and stories of prophets.

Listen to Surah Adh-Dhariyat (Verses 31-60)

Theme Division by Verses (Juz 27)

Verse Range Main Theme Core Message
Verses 31-37 Story of Prophet Lut and His People's Punishment Angels announce punishment for Lut's people; remaining signs of punishment as a lesson
Verses 38-46 Story of Moses, Pharaoh, and the Peoples of 'Ad & Thamud Proofs of Allah's power through Moses; destruction of Pharaoh, 'Ad, and Thamud due to disbelief
Verses 47-51 Proofs of Allah's Power in the Heavens and Earth Heavens built with power; earth spread out; creation in pairs; call to return to Allah
Verses 52-60 Warning and Conclusion Every messenger is accused of being a magician or mad; warning to those who deny; Allah is the Provider; threat of Hell's punishment

Message of Aqidah, Worship, & Akhlaq

  • Aqidah (Creed): Belief in Allah's punishment for deniers, faith that Allah created the heavens and earth with His power, conviction that only Allah is the Provider, rejection of accusations against messengers.
  • Worship: Run (strive) towards Allah, fear Him, worship only Allah.
  • Akhlak (Morality): Learn lessons from previous nations' stories, do not be arrogant like Pharaoh, do not deny the truth, realize that worldly life is only amusement and diversion.

Surah At-Tur (The Mount) - Verses 1-49

Overview of Surah At-Tur

Surah At-Tur is the 52nd chapter, consisting of 49 verses. The entire surah is in Juz 27. This Meccan surah affirms the certainty of the Day of Judgment, refutes the accusations of the polytheists against the Prophet, and provides proofs of Allah's oneness.

Verses 1-28 Certainty of the Day of Judgment and Recompense Allah's oaths by His creations; description of the Day of Judgment; Paradise blessings for the righteous; Hell punishment for deniers
Verses 29-34 Refutation of Accusations Against the Messenger Command to continue warning; refutation that the Prophet is not a magician or poet; challenge to produce a verse like the Qur'an

  • Aqidah (Creed): Absolute belief in the Day of Recompense, faith in the delights of Paradise and torment of Hell, conviction that the Qur'an is Allah's word, not poetry or magic, acknowledgment that the universe is created.
  • Worship: Glorify and praise your Lord at night and when the stars fade, be patient in facing mockery, continue to warn.
  • Akhlak (Morality): Do not be arrogant and deny the truth, reflect upon creation, emulate the Prophet's patience.

Overview of Surah An-Najm

Surah An-Najm is the 53rd chapter, consisting of 62 verses. The entire surah is in Juz 27. This Meccan surah defends the prophethood of Muhammad SAW from accusations of being astray, rejects idol worship, and affirms Allah's power.

  • Worship: Prostrate and worship only Allah, seek His forgiveness.
  • Akhlak (Morality): Avoid major sins and shameful deeds, do not follow conjecture, be humble by prostrating to Allah.

Surah Al-Qamar (The Moon) - Verses 1-55

Overview of Surah Al-Qamar

Surah Al-Qamar is the 54th chapter, consisting of 55 verses. The entire surah is in Juz 27. This Meccan surah presents a repeated pattern of warning: stories of former peoples who denied their messengers and how destructive punishment came upon them.

  • Worship: Take lessons from Allah's signs, remember and take warning.
  • Akhlak (Morality): Do not be arrogant and deny Allah's signs, hasten towards good, fear Allah's punishment.

Surah Ar-Rahman (The Most Merciful) - Verses 1-78

Overview of Surah Ar-Rahman

Surah Ar-Rahman is the 55th chapter, consisting of 78 verses. The entire surah is in Juz 27. This Meccan surah is famous for the repeated verse "So which of the favors of your Lord would you deny?" after mentioning various blessings of Allah.

Listen to Surah Ar-Rahman

Complete Text: reciteQuran.com - Surah Ar-Rahman

Theme Division by Verses

Verse Range Main Theme Core Message
Verses 1-13 Blessings of Creation and Teaching Allah taught the Qur'an; created man and taught him speech; created heaven, sun, moon, plants
Verses 14-25 Creation of Man, Jinn, and the Universe Man from clay; jinn from fire; creation of the two seas; Allah's power over all things
Verses 26-45 Transience of Creation and Threat for Transgressors Everything on earth will perish; only the Face of Allah remains; accountability for man and jinn; punishment for those who transgress
Verses 46-78 Description of Paradise Blessings Two Gardens for the righteous; beauty, palaces, houris, carpets; all delights never seen, heard, or imagined

Message of Aqidah, Worship, & Akhlaq

  • Aqidah (Creed): Acknowledgment that Allah is Ar-Rahman, belief that the Qur'an is taught by Him, faith in the Day of Account for man and jinn, conviction in the eternal delights of Paradise.
  • Worship: Constantly be grateful for all Allah's blessings (refrain verse "So which of the favors of your Lord would you deny?"), worship only Allah.
  • Akhlak (Morality): Acknowledge and do not deny Allah's favors, fear the account in the Hereafter, possess modesty.

Surah Al-Waqi'ah (The Inevitable Event) - Verses 1-96

Overview of Surah Al-Waqi'ah

Surah Al-Waqi'ah is the 56th chapter, consisting of 96 verses. The entire surah is in Juz 27. This Meccan surah classifies people on the Day of Judgment into three groups: As-Sabiqun (the foremost in faith), Ashhabul-Yamin (the companions of the right), and Ashhabush-Shimal (the companions of the left).

Listen to Surah Al-Waqi'ah

Complete Text: reciteQuran.com - Surah Al-Waqi'ah

Theme Division by Verses

Verse Range Main Theme Core Message
Verses 1-56 Description of the Day of Judgment and Three Human Groups State of the earth and mountains during the Hour; three groups of people with their respective recompense; Paradise delights for As-Sabiqun and Ashhabul-Yamin; Hell punishment for Ashhabush-Shimal
Verses 57-74 Proofs of Allah's Power in Creation Creation of man from semen; creation of plants and fire; the sanctity and greatness of your Lord's name
Verses 75-82 Oath upon the Nobility of the Qur'an Oath by the setting of the stars; the Qur'an is a noble recitation; condemnation for those who deny it
Verses 83-96 Description of the Agony of Death and Certainty of Resurrection The process of the agony of death; affirmation that Allah can revive decayed bones; reaffirmation of recompense for the two groups (right and left)

Message of Aqidah, Worship, & Akhlaq

  • Aqidah (Creed): Profound belief in the Day of Judgment, faith in the division of three human groups in the Hereafter, conviction in Allah's power to create man and the universe, acknowledgment that the Qur'an is a noble revelation.
  • Worship: Exalt the name of your Lord, the Most Great, recite the Qur'an.
  • Akhlak (Morality): Strive to be among As-Sabiqun, be grateful for the blessing of creation, do not deny the truth.

Surah Al-Hadid (Iron) - Verses 1-29

Overview of the Beginning of Surah Al-Hadid

Surah Al-Hadid is the 57th chapter, consisting of 29 verses, all in Juz 27. This Medinan surah emphasizes that everything glorifies Allah, the command to believe and spend, and the characteristics of hypocrites.

Listen to Surah Al-Hadid (Verses 1-29)

Complete Text: reciteQuran.com - Surah Al-Hadid

Theme Division by Verses

Verse Range Main Theme Core Message
Verses 1-6 Allah's Oneness and Absolute Power Everything glorifies Him; He is the First, the Last, the Manifest, the Hidden; He regulates all affairs
Verses 7-11 Call to Believe and Spend Command to believe in Allah and His Messenger; command to spend; multiplied reward for those who spend; condition of hypocrites
Verses 12-18 Light on the Day of Judgment and Worldly Life Description of the light of believers and hypocrites on Judgment Day; warning that worldly life is only play; call to repent
Verses 19-24 Reward for Believers and the Patient Those who believe and affirm the messengers are the truthful and martyrs; encouragement not to grieve over what is missed and not to be arrogant
Verses 25-29 Sending of Messengers and Encouragement of Asceticism Allah sent messengers with proofs and the Scripture; sent down iron; call to the People of the Book to believe; encouragement to live ascetically (monasticism)

Message of Aqidah, Worship, & Akhlaq

  • Aqidah (Creed): Belief that Allah is the First, the Last, the Manifest, and the Hidden, faith in the Day of Resurrection and accountability, conviction that iron was sent down by Allah with great benefits.
  • Worship: Glorify Allah, believe in Allah and His Messenger, spend in Allah's way, increase seeking forgiveness at dawn.
  • Akhlak (Morality): Do not be stingy, do not be arrogant, be patient, live ascetically towards the world, act justly.
