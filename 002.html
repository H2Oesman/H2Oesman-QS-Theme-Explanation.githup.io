Gambaran Umum Surah Al-Baqarah (Surah ke-2)

Surah ke-2 (286 ayat) Madaniyah. Menurut IslamicStudies.info, Surah Al-Baqarah adalah jawaban detail dari permohonan petunjuk dalam Surah Al-Fatihah. Surah ini mencakup seluruh aspek ajaran Islam secara komprehensif: akidah, ibadah, muamalah, keluarga, ekonomi, jihad, kisah-kisah umat terdahulu, dan petunjuk hidup individu serta masyarakat. Dinamakan "Al-Baqarah" (Sapi Betina) karena kisah dalam ayat 67-73 yang mengajarkan kepatuhan mutlak kepada Allah.

Konteks Penurunan

Surah Al-Baqarah diturunkan di Madinah setelah hijrah, sebagai respons terhadap kondisi masyarakat Madinah yang terdiri dari kaum Muslimin, Yahudi, dan munafik. Surah ini menjadi fondasi hukum dan tatanan sosial bagi negara Islam yang baru terbentuk.

Lokasi: Madinah (Madaniyah)
Periode: Awal hingga pertengahan periode Madinah
Latar Belakang: Pembentukan masyarakat Islam, dialog dengan Ahli Kitab, dan penetapan hukum
Tujuan: Petunjuk komprehensif bagi individu dan masyarakat
Akidah: Keyakinan bahwa Allah satu-satunya penentu halal dan haram. Islam adalah agama yang sempurna dan diridai Allah. Iman kepada semua kitab Allah dan para rasul. (QS 5:3, 5:48)
Ibadah: Perintah menepati janji dan perjanjian sebagai ibadah sosial. Ibadah haji, penyembelihan hewan, shalat, zakat, dan doa termasuk dalam pengaturan surah ini. (5:1, 5:5, 5:6)
Akhlak: Keadilan universal (5:8), tolong-menolong dalam kebaikan (5:2), menepati janji (5:1), larangan berlebihan dalam beragama (5:87), dan larangan mengambil loyalitas kepada musuh Islam yang memerangi agama.

Ayat Kunci untuk Tadabbur

ٱللَّهُ لَآ إِلَـٰهَ إِلَّا هُوَ ٱلْحَىُّ ٱلْقَيُّومُ ۚ لَا تَأْخُذُهُۥ سِنَةٌ وَلَا نَوْمٌ ۚ لَّهُۥ مَا فِى ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٰتِ وَمَا فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ ۗ مَن ذَا ٱلَّذِى يَشْفَعُ عِندَهُۥٓ إِلَّا بِإِذْنِهِۦ ۚ يَعْلَمُ مَا بَيْنَ أَيْدِيهِمْ وَمَا خَلْفَهُمْ ۖ وَلَا يُحِيطُونَ بِشَىْءٍ مِّنْ عِلْمِهِۦٓ إِلَّا بِمَا شَآءَ ۚ وَسِعَ كُرْسِيُّهُ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضَ ۖ وَلَا يَـُٔودُهُۥ حِفْظُهُمَا ۚ وَهُوَ ٱلْعَلِىُّ ٱلْعَظِيمُ ﴿٢٥٥﴾
Allāhu lā ilāha illā huw, al-ḥayyul-qayyụm, lā ta`khużuhụ sinatuw wa lā na`ụm, lahụ mā fis-samāwāti wa mā fil-arḍ, man żallażī yasyfa`u `indahū illā bi`iżnih, ya`lamu mā baina aidīhim wa mā khalfahum, wa lā yuḥīṭụna bisyai`im min `ilmihī illā bimā syā`, wasi`a kursiyyuhus-samāwāti wal-arḍ, wa lā ya`ụduhụ ḥifẓuhumā, wa huwal-'aliyyul-'aẓīm.
Terjemahan: "Allah, tidak ada Tuhan selain Dia, Yang Maha Hidup, Yang terus-menerus mengurus (makhluk-Nya). Tidak mengantuk dan tidak tidur. Milik-Nya apa yang di langit dan apa yang di bumi. Tidak ada yang dapat memberi syafaat di sisi-Nya tanpa izin-Nya. Dia mengetahui apa yang di hadapan mereka dan apa yang di belakang mereka, dan mereka tidak mengetahui sesuatu apa pun tentang ilmu-Nya melainkan apa yang Dia kehendaki. Kursi-Nya meliputi langit dan bumi. Dan Dia tidak merasa berat memelihara keduanya, dan Dia Maha Tinggi, Maha Besar."
Tafsir IslamicStudies.info: Ayat paling agung dalam Al-Qur'an. Barangsiapa membacanya setelah shalat, tidak ada yang menghalanginya masuk surga kecuali kematian (HR. An-Nasa'i). Pelindung dari setan jika dibaca sebelum tidur (HR. Al-Bukhari). 📖 Baca tafsir lengkap di IslamicStudies.info Klik Detail
Tafsir Ibnu Katsir: Ayat Kursi merupakan ayat paling agung dalam Al-Qur'an. Di dalamnya terkandung Ismullah al-A'ẓam (nama Allah yang teragung). "Al-Ḥayy" (Maha Hidup) mencakup kesempurnaan hidup mutlak, dan "Al-Qayyūm" (Maha Berdiri Sendiri) mencakup kekuasaan mutlak Allah atas seluruh makhluk. Kursi Allah meliputi langit dan bumi, dan memelihara keduanya tidak memberatkan-Nya sedikit pun. 📖 Baca tafsir Ibnu Katsir selengkapnya

Keutamaan Khusus Surah Al-Baqarah

  • Puncak Al-Qur'an (Sanamul Qur'an): Setan lari dari rumah yang dibaca di dalamnya surah Al-Baqarah (HR. Muslim).
  • Ayat Kursi (2:255): Perlindungan terbesar, ayat paling agung.
  • Dua ayat terakhir (285-286): Cukup bagi seorang muslim yang membacanya pada malam hari (HR. Bukhari).
  • Petunjuk komprehensif: Akidah, ibadah, muamalah, keluarga, ekonomi, jihad, kisah umat terdahulu.
  • Benteng dari sihir dan gangguan: Membacanya secara rutin mengusir setan.

Hikmah Surah Al-Baqarah

Induk Hukum Islam

Mencakup hukum ibadah, muamalah, dan pidana.

Umat Pertengahan

Konsep wasathiyah sebagai jalan moderat.

Kisah-kisah Teladan

Ibrahim, Musa, Adam, Thalut – pelajaran berharga.

Ekonomi Syariah

Larangan riba dan prinsip utang-piutang.

1. Karakter Orang Beriman - Ayat 1-5
الٓمٓ ﴿١﴾
ذَٰلِكَ ٱلْكِتَـٰبُ لَا رَيْبَ ۛ فِيهِ ۛ هُدًى لِّلْمُتَّقِينَ ﴿٢﴾
ٱلَّذِينَ يُؤْمِنُونَ بِٱلْغَيْبِ وَيُقِيمُونَ ٱلصَّلَوٰةَ وَمِمَّا رَزَقْنَـٰهُمْ يُنفِقُونَ ﴿٣﴾
وَٱلَّذِينَ يُؤْمِنُونَ بِمَآ أُنزِلَ إِلَيْكَ وَمَآ أُنزِلَ مِن قَبْلِكَ وَبِٱلْـَٔاخِرَةِ هُمْ يُوقِنُونَ ﴿٤﴾
أُو۟لَـٰٓئِكَ عَلَىٰ هُدًى مِّن رَّبِّهِمْ ۖ وَأُو۟لَـٰٓئِكَ هُمُ ٱلْمُفْلِحُونَ ﴿٥﴾
Alif-Lām-Mīm. (1) Dzālikal-kitābu lā raiba fīh, hudal-lil-muttaqīn. (2) Allażīna yu`minụna bil-gaibi wa yuqīmụnaṣ-ṣalāta wa mimmā razaqnāhum yunfiqụn. (3) Wallażīna yu`minụna bimā unzila ilaika wa mā unzila ming qablik, wa bil-ākhirati hum yụqinụn. (4) Ulā`ika `alā hudam mir rabbihim wa ulā`ika humul-mufliḥụn. (5)
(1) Alif Lam Mim. (2) Kitab ini tidak ada keraguan padanya; petunjuk bagi mereka yang bertakwa. (3) (yaitu) mereka yang beriman kepada yang gaib, melaksanakan shalat, dan menginfakkan sebagian rezeki yang Kami berikan kepada mereka. (4) dan mereka yang beriman kepada (Al-Qur'an) yang diturunkan kepadamu (Muhammad) dan kitab-kitab yang telah diturunkan sebelumnya, dan mereka yakin akan adanya akhirat. (5) Merekalah yang tetap mendapat petunjuk dari Tuhannya, dan mereka itulah orang-orang yang beruntung.
Tafsir IslamicStudies.info: Lima karakter orang bertakwa: iman kepada yang gaib, shalat, infak, iman kepada kitab-kitab sebelumnya, dan yakin akhirat. 📖 Baca tafsir lengkap Klik Detail
Tafsir Ibnu Katsir: "Alif Lam Mim" termasuk huruf muqaṭṭa'ah yang hanya Allah mengetahui hakikatnya. Al-Qur'an adalah petunjuk bagi orang bertakwa. Iman kepada yang gaib adalah fondasi seluruh amal. 📖 Baca tafsir Ibnu Katsir selengkapnya
2. Karakter Orang Kafir - Ayat 6-7
إِنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ سَوَآءٌ عَلَيْهِمْ ءَأَنذَرْتَهُمْ أَمْ لَمْ تُنذِرْهُمْ لَا يُؤْمِنُونَ ﴿٦﴾

Tafsir Ibnu Katsir: Riba (bunga) adalah kelebihan pembayaran tanpa adanya barang yang setara. Allah menghalalkan jual beli dan mengharamkan riba.
14. Ayat Kursi – Ayat 255
ٱللَّهُ لَآ إِلَـٰهَ إِلَّا هُوَ ٱلْحَىُّ ٱلْقَيُّومُ ...
Allah, tidak ada Tuhan selain Dia, Yang Maha Hidup, Yang terus-menerus mengurus (makhluk-Nya)...
Tafsir IslamicStudies.info: Ayat paling agung dalam Al-Qur'an. 📖 Baca tafsir lengkap Ayat Kursi 🔗
Tafsir Ibnu Katsir: Ayat Kursi mengandung nama Allah yang paling agung (Ismullah al-A'ẓam).
15. Iman Rasul dan Doa Penutup – Ayat 285–286
ءَامَنَ ٱلرَّسُولُ بِمَآ أُنزِلَ إِلَيْهِ مِن رَّبِّهِۦ ...
(285) Rasul telah beriman kepada apa yang diturunkan kepadanya dari Tuhannya, demikian pula orang-orang yang beriman. ... (286) Allah tidak membebani seseorang melainkan sesuai dengan kesanggupannya.
Tafsir IslamicStudies.info: Penutup agung yang mengajarkan keimanan sempurna dan doa ampunan. 📖 Baca tafsir lengkap 🔗
Tafsir Ibnu Katsir: Dua ayat terakhir ini sangat agung. Barangsiapa membacanya pada malam hari, maka sudah cukup baginya.

Surah Al-Baqarah (2) - Thematic Tafsir

Primary Tafsir Source: IslamicStudies.info - Surah Al-Baqarah (1-286) Click Details
بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَـٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ
Bismillāhir-raḥmānir-raḥīm
الٓمٓ ﴿١﴾ ذَٰلِكَ ٱلْكِتَـٰبُ لَا رَيْبَ ۛ فِيهِ ۛ هُدًى لِّلْمُتَّقِينَ ﴿٢﴾
"Alif Lam Mim. This is the Book about which there is no doubt, a guidance for those conscious of Allah." (2:1-2)

Overview of Surah Al-Baqarah (Surah 2)

Madani surah (286 verses). According to IslamicStudies.info, it is the detailed answer to the supplication for guidance in Al-Fatihah, covering all aspects of Islam: creed, worship, transactions, family law, economics, jihad, and stories of previous nations. Named "Al-Baqarah" (The Cow) after the story in verses 67-73 teaching absolute obedience to Allah.

Revelation Context

Revealed in Medina after the Hijrah, addressing the multi‑faith society of Muslims, Jews, and hypocrites. It laid the foundation for Islamic law and social order.

Location: Medina (Madani)
Period: Early to middle Medinan period
Background: Establishment of Islamic society, dialogue with People of the Book, legal rulings
Purpose: Comprehensive guidance for individuals and community
Aqidah (Creed): Belief that Allah alone determines halal and haram. Islam is the perfect and approved religion. Faith in all scriptures and messengers. (Q 5:3, 5:48)
Ibadah (Worship): Command to fulfill covenants as social worship. Hajj, animal slaughter, prayer, zakah, and supplication are regulated. (5:1, 5:5, 5:6)
Akhlaq (Character): Universal justice (5:8), cooperation in goodness (5:2), fulfilling promises (5:1), prohibition of excess in religion (5:87), and prohibition of loyalty to hostile enemies of Islam.

Special Significance

  • Called the "peak of the Qur'an" (Sanamul Qur'an). Satan flees from a house where it is recited (Muslim).
  • Ayat al-Kursi (2:255) is the greatest verse.
  • The last two verses (2:285-286) suffice a Muslim who recites them at night (Bukhari).
  • Comprehensive guidance: creed, worship, transactions, family, jihad, and stories.
  • Protection from sorcery and evil whispers when recited regularly.

Wisdom of Surah Al-Baqarah

Source of Islamic Law

Encompasses worship, transactions, and criminal law.

The Middle Nation

Concept of wasatiyyah – moderation.

Exemplary Stories

Ibrahim, Musa, Adam, Talut – valuable lessons.

Islamic Economics

Prohibition of riba and principles of debt.

Main Themes (Detailed Tafsir) – Full Surah

1. Characteristics of the Righteous – Verses 1–5
الٓمٓ ﴿١﴾
ذَٰلِكَ ٱلْكِتَـٰبُ لَا رَيْبَ ۛ فِيهِ ۛ هُدًى لِّلْمُتَّقِينَ ﴿٢﴾ ...
(1) Alif Lām Mīm. (2) This is the Book about which there is no doubt, a guidance for those conscious of Allah. (3) Who believe in the unseen, establish prayer, and spend out of what We have provided for them... (5) Those are upon [right] guidance from their Lord, and it is those who are the successful.
Tafsir IslamicStudies.info: Five characteristics of the righteous: faith in the unseen, prayer, charity, belief in all scriptures, certainty of the Hereafter. 📖 Full Tafsir 🔗
Tafsir Ibn Kathir: "Alif Lam Mim" are among the muqaṭṭa‘āt whose full meaning is known only to Allah. The Qur'an is guidance for the righteous. Faith in the unseen is the foundation.
2. Characteristics of the Disbelievers – Verses 6–7
إِنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ سَوَآءٌ عَلَيْهِمْ ءَأَنذَرْتَهُمْ أَمْ لَمْ تُنذِرْهُمْ لَا يُؤْمِنُونَ ﴿٦﴾ ...
(6) Indeed, those who disbelieve – it is all the same for them whether you warn them or do not warn them – they will not believe. (7) Allah has set a seal upon their hearts and upon their hearing, and over their vision is a veil. And for them is a great punishment.
Tafsir IslamicStudies.info: Stubborn disbelievers will not believe even if warned; their hearts are sealed as a consequence of persistent rejection. 📖 Full Tafsir 🔗
Tafsir Ibn Kathir: The sealing of hearts and hearing is not oppression from Allah but a recompense for their disbelief and refusal to accept the truth.
3. Characteristics of the Hypocrites – Verses 8–20
وَمِنَ ٱلنَّاسِ مَن يَقُولُ ءَامَنَّا بِٱللَّهِ وَبِٱلْيَوْمِ ٱلْـَٔاخِرِ وَمَا هُم بِمُؤْمِنِينَ ﴿٨﴾ ...
(8) And of the people are some who say, "We believe in Allah and the Last Day," but they are not believers. (9) They [think to] deceive Allah and those who believe, but they deceive not except themselves and perceive [it] not. ... (20) The lightning almost snatches away their sight... Indeed, Allah is over all things competent.
Tafsir IslamicStudies.info: Hypocrites claim faith but have diseased hearts. They deceive themselves and will have a painful punishment. 📖 Full Tafsir 🔗
Tafsir Ibn Kathir: "Disease in their hearts" is doubt and hypocrisy. Allah increases their disease because they persist in falsehood.
4. Story of the Creation of Adam – Verses 30–39
وَإِذْ قَالَ رَبُّكَ لِلْمَلَـٰٓئِكَةِ إِنِّى جَاعِلٌ فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ خَلِيفَةً ...
(30) And [mention, O Muhammad], when your Lord said to the angels, "Indeed, I will make upon the earth a successive authority." ... (39) And those who disbelieve and deny Our signs – those will be companions of the Fire; they will abide therein eternally.
Tafsir IslamicStudies.info: Humans as vicegerents on earth, the angels' dialogue shows human potential for good and evil, and the lesson of Iblis's arrogance. 📖 Full Tafsir 🔗
Tafsir Ibn Kathir: The angels did not know the wisdom behind creating humans. But Allah knows best that prophets, righteous people, and obedient servants will emerge from them.
5. Story of the Cow – Verses 67–73
وَإِذْ قَالَ مُوسَىٰ لِقَوْمِهِ إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ يَأْمُرُكُمْ أَن تَذْبَحُوا۟ بَقَرَةً ...
(67) And [recall] when Moses said to his people, "Indeed, Allah commands you to slaughter a cow." ... (73) So We said, "Strike him with part of it." Thus does Allah bring the dead to life, and He shows you His signs that you might reason.
Tafsir IslamicStudies.info: Absolute obedience without excessive questioning; the more they asked, the harder the command became. 📖 Full Tafsir 🔗
Tafsir Ibn Kathir: This story teaches the danger of excessive questioning and arguing. Obedience without excessive questioning is better.
6. Change of Qiblah – Verses 142–150
سَيَقُولُ ٱلسُّفَهَآءُ مِنَ ٱلنَّاسِ مَا وَلَّىٰهُمْ عَن قِبْلَتِهِمُ ...
(142) The foolish among the people will say, "What has turned them away from their qiblah?" ... (150) And from wherever you go out, turn your face toward al-Masjid al-Haram. ... so that you may be guided.
Tafsir IslamicStudies.info: A test of faith to distinguish true believers from hypocrites, and the restoration of the Ka'bah as the center of tawhid. 📖 Full Tafsir 🔗
Tafsir Ibn Kathir: The change of qiblah was a test for the hypocrites. The believers accepted it with full obedience.
7. Obligation of Fasting – Verses 183–187
يَـٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ كُتِبَ عَلَيْكُمُ ٱلصِّيَامُ ...
(183) O you who have believed, decreed upon you is fasting as it was decreed upon those before you that you may become righteous. ... (187) ... These are the limits [set by] Allah, so do not approach them. Thus does Allah make clear His ordinances to the people that they may become righteous.
Tafsir IslamicStudies.info: Fasting is prescribed to attain taqwa (God‑consciousness), train self‑control, and remind of the suffering of the poor. 📖 Full Tafsir 🔗
Tafsir Ibn Kathir: Fasting trains the soul to abandon desires for the sake of Allah's pleasure. It also cultivates empathy for the poor.
8. Prophet Ibrahim and the Building of the Ka'bah – Verses 124–129
وَإِذِ ٱبْتَلَىٰٓ إِبْرَٰهِـۧمَ رَبُّهُۥ بِكَلِمَـٰتٍۢ ...
(124) And [mention, O Muhammad], when Abraham was tried by his Lord with commands and he fulfilled them. ... (129) "Our Lord, and send among them a messenger from themselves who will recite to them Your verses..."
Tafsir IslamicStudies.info: Ibrahim was made an imam after passing severe tests. The Ka'bah was built as the center of tawhid. 📖 Full Tafsir 🔗
Tafsir Ibn Kathir: Ibrahim's tests included the command to sacrifice his son, migration, and various severe trials. When he passed, Allah made him a leader for all humanity.
9. Law of Qisas – Verses 178–179
يَـٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ كُتِبَ عَلَيْكُمُ ٱلْقِصَاصُ ...
(178) O you who have believed, prescribed for you is legal retribution for those murdered – the free for the free, the slave for the slave... (179) And there is for you in legal retribution [saving of] life, O you [people] of understanding, that you may become righteous.
Tafsir IslamicStudies.info: Qisas is justice that preserves life. Forgiveness is better, but proportionate punishment prevents crime. 📖 Full Tafsir 🔗
Tafsir Ibn Kathir: Qisas preserves life because the existence of capital punishment makes potential murderers think twice.
10. Command of Jihad – Verses 190–195
وَقَـٰتِلُوا۟ فِى سَبِيلِ ٱللَّهِ ٱلَّذِينَ يُقَـٰتِلُونَكُمْ ...
(190) Fight in the way of Allah those who fight you but do not transgress. Indeed, Allah does not like transgressors. ... (195) And spend in the way of Allah and do not throw [yourselves] with your [own] hands into destruction. And do good; indeed, Allah loves the doers of good.
Tafsir IslamicStudies.info: Jihad is only for self‑defense and establishing justice; transgression is forbidden. 📖 Full Tafsir 🔗
Tafsir Ibn Kathir: These verses explain the ethics of war in Islam: only fight those who fight you, do not kill women, children, the elderly, monks, and do not destroy crops.
11. Hajj and Umrah – Verses 196–203
وَأَتِمُّوا۟ ٱلْحَجَّ وَٱلْعُمْرَةَ لِلَّهِ ...
(196) And complete the Hajj and ‘umrah for Allah. ... (203) And remember Allah during [specific] numbered days. Then whoever hastens [his departure] in two days – there is no sin upon him... and know that you will be gathered to Him.
Tafsir IslamicStudies.info: Command to complete Hajj and ‘umrah, concessions for the sick or those who are prevented. 📖 Full Tafsir 🔗
Tafsir Ibn Kathir: Hajj is a pillar of Islam obligatory for those who are able. This verse explains the expiation (dam) for those who are prevented or violate the state of ihram.
12. Marriage, Divorce, and Waiting Period – Verses 221–242
وَلَا تَنكِحُوا۟ ٱلْمُشْرِكَـٰتِ حَتَّىٰ يُؤْمِنَّ ...
(221) Do not marry polytheistic women until they believe... (228) Divorced women shall wait concerning themselves for three menstrual periods... (242) Thus does Allah make clear to you His verses that you might reason.
Tafsir IslamicStudies.info: Prohibition of marrying polytheists, the virtue of marrying a believer, and rules of divorce, waiting period, and maintenance. 📖 Full Tafsir 🔗
Tafsir Ibn Kathir: Marriage to polytheists is forbidden because it will negatively affect the religion of children and the family. A believing slave is better than a free polytheist.
13. Prohibition of Usury (Riba) – Verses 275–279
ٱلَّذِينَ يَأْكُلُونَ ٱلرِّبَوٰا۟ لَا يَقُومُونَ إِلَّا كَمَا يَقُومُ ٱلَّذِى يَتَخَبَّطُهُ ٱلشَّيْطَـٰنُ ...
(275) Those who consume interest cannot stand [on the Day of Resurrection] except as one stands who is being beaten by Satan into insanity... (279) And if you do not, then be informed of a war [against you] from Allah and His Messenger. But if you repent, you may have your principal – [thus] you do no wrong, nor are you wronged.
Tafsir IslamicStudies.info: Riba is a major sin declared war by Allah and His Messenger. Those who repent are forgiven, but those who persist will be in Hellfire forever. 📖 Full Tafsir 🔗
Tafsir Ibn Kathir: Riba (interest) is any excess payment without a counter‑value. Allah permitted trade because it has mutual benefit, and prohibited riba because it contains injustice.
14. Ayat al-Kursi – Verse 255
ٱللَّهُ لَآ إِلَـٰهَ إِلَّا هُوَ ٱلْحَىُّ ٱلْقَيُّومُ ...
Allah – there is no deity except Him, the Ever‑Living, the Self‑Sustaining. Neither drowsiness overtakes Him nor sleep... He is the Most High, the Most Great.
Tafsir IslamicStudies.info: The greatest verse of the Qur'an. Reciting it after prayers guarantees nothing prevents one from entering Paradise except death; reciting before sleeping protects from Satan until morning. 📖 Full Tafsir of Ayat al-Kursi 🔗
Tafsir Ibn Kathir: Ayat al-Kursi contains Allah’s greatest name (Ismullah al-A’ẓam). “Al-Ḥayy” (the Ever‑Living) encompasses all perfection of life, and “Al-Qayyūm” (the Self‑Sustaining) encompasses absolute sovereignty over all creation.
15. The Messenger’s Faith and Closing Supplication – Verses 285–286
ءَامَنَ ٱلرَّسُولُ بِمَآ أُنزِلَ إِلَيْهِ مِن رَّبِّهِۦ ...
(285) The Messenger has believed in what was revealed to him from his Lord, and [so have] the believers... (286) Allah does not charge a soul except [with that within] its capacity... "Our Lord, do not impose blame upon us if we have forgotten or erred... You are our protector, so give us victory over the disbelieving people."
Tafsir IslamicStudies.info: A magnificent conclusion teaching perfect faith, the attitude of "we hear and we obey," a prayer for forgiveness, and the principle that Allah does not burden a soul beyond its capacity. 📖 Full Tafsir 🔗
Tafsir Ibn Kathir: These last two verses are extremely great. Whoever recites them at night will be sufficiently protected (Bukhari).
